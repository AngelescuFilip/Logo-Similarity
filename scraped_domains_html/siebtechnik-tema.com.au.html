SIEBTECHNIK TEMA is part of a globally active, family-owned group of companies.

We are experts in the field of solid-liquid separation and processing of bulk materials.

As a renowned OEM & system supplier, our focus is on the decanter and centrifuge business, supplemented by an extensive range of screening and jigging machines, sampling systems and laboratory equipment. In addition, we sell special machines and systems as well as selected products from our group of companies in various regions.

Customized solutions, quality and reliability have been the key to our success for over 100 years.

Ammonium Sulfate

Ammonium sulfate is mainly used as a nitrogen fertilizer in agriculture. There are also many other niche applications, such as food additives and drinking water treatment.

Boric Acid

Boric acid is an inorganic acid. It is often used as an antiseptic, insecticide, flame retardant, neutron absorber or as a precursor for other chemical compounds.

Cellulose Ether

A typical product for SIEBTECHNIK TEMA centrifuges are cellulose ether derivatives. For more than 60 years, these versatile products have played an important role in a wide range of applications, from building products, ceramics and paints to food, cosmetics and pharmaceuticals.

Read more

Glycerin (Pharma)

Glycerin is an organic compound belonging to the sugar alcohol group. It is used in a variety of industries, including food, cosmetics and medicine.

Lithium

We know what we are talking about when it comes to lithium processing and offer efficient solutions for the gentle processing of lithium derivatives. The focus is on continuous and discontinuous centrifuges and dryers that have been specifically designed to wash and dewater various lithium compounds.

Polyethylene, or PE for short, is the most widely used thermoplastic in our society and an essential part of modern life.

Polyvinyl Chloride (PVC)

Pulp & Paper

Sodium bicarbonate

Sodium hydrogen carbonate (NaHCO₃) is a fine, white, crystalline powder that is often used as a leavening agent. Due to the fineness of the crystals, our SHS pusher centrifuge is particularly suitable for the post-dehydration of baking soda.

Sampling

Crushing

We offer a wide range of solutions in the field of coarse, fine and ultrafine comminution of raw materials, chemically/thermally produced materials and in the recycling of valuable materials. In addition to pure particle size reduction, our extensive product portfolio can also be used to produce specific particle shapes and distributions. We also offer solutions for the mechanical activation of materials.

Read more

Analyzing

We manufacture: Analytical Screening machines for determining the grain size of bulk materials of all grain sizes. Test drums for determining drum strength. Gas pycnometers for determining skeletal density. Robot cells for automatic sample preparation and analysis of physical parameters. Dividers for representative rejuvenation of sample quantities and Laboratory Centrifuges for separating solids from suspensions.

Read more

Screening

Our screening machines are suitable for filtering, screening and classifying liquids and solids in a wide range of industrial processes. Reliable and clean separation of heavy, light, large, small, dry, dusty or even wet, sticky materials is always guaranteed.

Read more

Sorting

Our jigging machines are extremely versatile and have proven themselves in the separation of impurities from materials such as sand, gravel, slag, etc.
Our semi-mobile pulsator jig offers a practical solution, especially in situations where time constraints make the construction of a fixed plant uneconomical. It can be used on site and maximizes the efficiency of your sorting process.

Filtering

Separating, clarifying, cleaning with centrifuges and filters. Filtration solutions for high product quality, sustainability and resource efficiency.
