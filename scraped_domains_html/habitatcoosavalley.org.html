About Us

Habitat for Humanity-Coosa Valley seeks to reflect God's love by helping partner families achieve safe, decent and affordable housing, while providing opportunities for community partners to use their God-given gifts. 

Our Service 

Habitat for Humanity-Coosa Valley has been helping families in our community since 1984.  We have helped over 150 families by building and rehabbing houses. Qualifying families purchase a safe, decent, affordable home through a zero-interest loan extended by Habitat. Families must also contribute 250 - 300 hours of "sweat equity," working on their home and helping Habitat in other ways. In this way, Habitat is "a hand up, not a handout."

Donating

Help Habitat for Humanity by sending a gift to continue our mission.  Your gift can be sent in honor or memory of a loved one. You can mail or bring your donation to our office located at 504 E First Ave, Rome, GA  30161,  9 - 4:30pm, Tue - Fri or 9 - 1:30pm Saturday.  If you would like to make your donation online, simply click on the "Donate" at the top of the page. Your gift will help a family to have a happy and stable life in a safe, decent, and affordable home for years to come.
