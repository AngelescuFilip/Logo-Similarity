About us

Smart so­lu­tions are our spe­cialty

Mak­ing in­dus­trial au­toma­tion as sim­ple, seam­less and cost-ef­fi­cient as pos­si­ble with smart so­lu­tions: Mur­relek­tronik is the fore­run­ner in de­cen­tral­ized elec­tri­cal au­toma­tion tech­nol­ogy and has be­come, based on this DNA, the lead­ing com­pany in this field.

Our so­lu­tions bring sig­nals, data and power close to the process, elim­i­nat­ing the need for con­trol cab­i­nets

For 50 years, more and more cus­tomers world­wide from all man­u­fac­tur­ing in­dus­tries have placed their trust in this spe­cial ex­per­tise.

We have an ex­ten­sive net­work of over 3,200 ex­perts in more than 50 coun­tries. With sev­eral global pro­duc­tion and lo­gis­tics lo­ca­tions, we are close to our cus­tomers to solve their di­verse chal­lenges quickly and smartly. Our port­fo­lio cov­ers a wide range – from con­nec­tors to I/O sys­tems, power sup­plies and net­work tech­nol­ogy up to the fast and easy in­stal­lable, mod­u­lar com­plete so­lu­tion for cab­i­net-free au­toma­tion, called Vario-X.

As part of our plug and play mind­set, we also offer holis­tic dig­i­tal ser­vices sup­port­ing in­stal­la­tion, com­mis­sion­ing and main­te­nance.
