Welcome to Webster Leiden

About Webster Leiden Campus

At Webster Leiden, you will become a lifelong independent learner and leader, gaining the necessary knowledge and skills to succeed in today's world. Webster Leiden is an international campus of Webster University, an accredited, private, nonprofit American university.

Achieve your academic and professional goals through our challenging and in-demand programs. Built to provide flexibility and convenience, earn a degree at your own pace.

Our class sizes are small, so you’ll receive individual attention and mentorship from our faculty, who bring real-life knowledge to the classroom.
