Why choose the Implantcenter?

COMPLEX DENTAL TREATMENTS

We provide a full range of dental and oral surgical services in the fields of general dentistry,  cosmetic dentistry, implantology and orthodontics for our patients. Experince our professional, painless treatments in a calm and friendly environment, at affordable price.

TEAM OF PROFESSIONAL SPECIALISTS

Our dental team is composed of internationally trained professionals with several years of experience. Dental services provided at the highest level of dental standard by our dentists and specialists of oral surgery and orhodontics are at your service.

WE MASTER IMPLANTOLOGY

Dental implants can restore both the look and the function of the tooth giving back the natural comfort and the pleasure of eating for patients. Our Implantologists carry out implant treatments according to the leading technology and standards of implantation and use the highest quality, internationally recognized dental implant brands.

EXPERIENCED ORTHODONTISTS, GREAT CHOICE OF QUALITY BRANDS

Dental braces and orthodontic treatment are used to correct “bad bites,” or malocclusion. At Implantcenter you can choose among different types of external and invisible technical solutions provided by well-known brands like Invisalign®, Inspire ICE™, Damon Clear, Lotus™.

NO WAITING LIST, SHORTEST POSSIBLE TREATMENT TIME

At Implantcenter, there is no waiting list. Our team is to help you achieve the smile you wish within the shortest treatment time possible. Our strengths include personalised treatment time management and the carefully planned and executed dental treatment plan.
