DYNAMIC SHIELD

Drive with confidence and security

The front of the car doesn’t just create a daring and unique face that can be seen on all Mitsubishi Motors vehicles. It’s also designed to create a feeling of protection and improve performance. We call it the DYNAMIC SHIELD.

12.3-INCH FULL DIGITAL DRIVER DISPLAY

Putting you in control on the road

The full Digital Driver Display reinvents the space where the speedometer and rev counter live and turns it into an info hub. Choose one mode and it displays a traditional rev counter and speedo. Choose another mode and you’ll get an enriched display showing the 4WD status or the navigation map and more. Just select your preferred contents – it’s all controlled from a switch on the steering wheel.
