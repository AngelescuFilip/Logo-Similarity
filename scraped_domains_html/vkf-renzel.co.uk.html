VKF RENZEL - THE UK'S NO.1 POS SUPPLIER

  
VKF Renzel (UK) Ltd is one of the UK’s leading suppliers and manufacturers of acrylic and PVC products in the POS industry.

Our in-house manufacturing facility in Worcestershire uses state of the art machinery to realise your ideas, and our warehouse holds stock of many of the most popular items, which in turn enables VKF Renzel to ship products much quicker than our competitors.

The team in Worcestershire has a wealth of experience in the display market and our production staff only works to the highest standards as you, our customer, would expect. VKF Renzel (UK) Ltd also benefits from being part of the VKF Renzel group located in more than 20 countries throughout the world, making us the perfect partner in sourcing goods globally or drawing on our worldwide manufacturing facilities in various materials.

Bespoke Products

With the advanced technologies we have here at VKF Renzel, we are able to produce almost any product! Bespoke products are one of our specialities and we have worked with many worldwide, established brands to create perfect and effective display stands.

Whether it be wood, acrylic, PVC or LEDs, we are certain we can create the best solution for you.

Our dedicated team are able to assist with the design and overall look of your product to create a stunning bespoke stand for your business. If you wish to speak to us about creating your own custom acrylic display stand, please call us on 01527 878311 or email sales@vkf-renzel.co.uk.
