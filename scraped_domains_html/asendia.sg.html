We're proud to offer 100% carbon neutral solutions through offsetting

As one of the largest global logistics providers, we know that protecting our planet comes first.

Since January 2022, Asendia has been 100% Carbon Neutral by offsetting all our international and local transport emissions, including those by our first-mile and last-mile partners. We'd like to go even further, so will be joining the well-known Science-Based Target initiative (SBTi) and committing to set science-based targets before the end of 2024.
