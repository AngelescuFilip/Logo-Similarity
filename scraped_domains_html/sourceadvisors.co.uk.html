What we do

We create value for our clients by fully recognising their R&D and maximising the value of their Intellectual Property. R&D tax credits

In the UK companies are able to claim tax relief for their R&D activity. With our thorough understanding of the R&D ecosystem, our technical capability and our compliance led approach, we create value to our clients by identifying their R&D and creating a robust claim.

R&D and Patent Box tax relief is available to companies in many sectors

Technology, information and communication

Companies in this sector are focussed on telecommunications, computer programming and consultancy, as well as information service activities including data processing, hosting and related activities.

Read more

Professionals and science

When we think of R&D activity it could well be that scientific or engineering firms come to mind. This sector includes pharmaceutical and biotech activity, alongside many other  businesses that have R&D at their core.

Read more

Manufacturing

Their R&D activity in Manufacturing companies stretches from product development, production line speed and resource improvement, quality control and compliance.

Read more

Construction

Increasingly construction businesses are innovating in their use of green or sustainable methods. R&D often leads to an improvement in existing construction techniques to solve site specific or environmental issues.

Read more

Agriculture, forestry & fishing

In this sector innovation can come from process improvements, production improvement and scalability and quality control. Many agricultural businesses are building new processes to reduce waste or reuse waste products in novel ways.

Finance and Insurance

Innovative businesses in this sector include financial service activities such as insurance, reinsurance and pension funding activities, and business activities to support financial services.
