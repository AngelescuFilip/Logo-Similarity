Our core values

  • Sustainability

    We are committed to sustainability in all aspects of our operations, from project development to ongoing management. We prioritize renewable energy solutions that minimize environmental impact and maximize social and economic benefits.

  • Integrity

    We conduct our business with the highest standards of integrity, honesty, and transparency. We build trust with our clients, partners, and stakeholders through open communication, ethical practices, and accountability.

  • Innovation

    We embrace innovation as a driving force for progress and change. We continuously seek new technologies, methodologies, and solutions to improve the efficiency, effectiveness, and sustainability of our projects.

  • Collaboration

    We believe in the power of collaboration and partnership to achieve shared goals. We work closely with our clients, suppliers, communities, and industry stakeholders to create value and drive meaningful impact.

  • Empowerment

    We empower our employees to make a difference and contribute to our mission. We foster a culture of empowerment, diversity, and inclusion, where everyone has the opportunity to thrive and succeed.

"At Soventix, sustainability is more than just a buzzword – it's at the heart of everything we do. From developing innovative solar solutions to promoting environmental conservation and social responsibility, we are dedicated to creating a sustainable future for generations to come."
Thorsten Preugschas
Group CEO
