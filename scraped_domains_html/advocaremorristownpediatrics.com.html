Welcome to Advocare Morristown Pediatric Associates

We are proud to have been serving the community for over fifty years, providing exceptional pediatric care with a focus on personalized service and patient satisfaction. Founded in 1972 by Dr. Martin L. Cohen, M.D., our practice continues to thrive under the dedication of a talented team of medical professionals. Dr. Cohen’s legacy of compassionate care lives on as we strive to provide the highest level of healthcare for every child.


Our team includes a distinguished group of providers, including Dr. Suhaib Nashi, Dr. Tamara McCluskey, Dr. Gwyn Poblete, Dr. Karolina Janasek, Dr. Erin Cahill, Nurse Practitioner Nicole Vales-Buckwald, and Nurse Practitioner Josefina Gregory. Together, our five board-certified pediatricians and skilled nurse practitioners work hand-in-hand to nurture the health and well-being of children from infancy through adolescence.


At Advocare Morristown Pediatric Associates, we are committed to staying at the forefront of pediatric care by utilizing the latest advancements in medical research and technology. We offer a comprehensive range of services, including preventive care, treatment for acute and chronic illnesses, support for developmental and behavioral concerns, and guidance for adolescent health issues. Our team is dedicated to providing holistic care that meets the unique needs of each child, while always fostering open communication with parents.


We believe that strong partnerships with families are essential in delivering the best care. Our team takes the time to listen to your concerns and works collaboratively with specialists when needed to ensure that your child receives the best possible treatment.


To make your visit as comfortable as possible, we offer two welcoming waiting areas at our Medical Condominium on James Street—one for sick patients and one for good patients. Both waiting areas are spacious with separate entrances for added convenience. Additionally, our office includes a modern laboratory/nursing station and eleven examination rooms. We are conveniently located near I-287 with ample parking.


We look forward to welcoming you to Advocare Morristown Pediatric Associates. Please contact us to schedule an appointment, and remember that all office visits are by appointment only to ensure personalized care. Choosing us means choosing a caring, professional, and supportive environment for your child's health and well-being.
