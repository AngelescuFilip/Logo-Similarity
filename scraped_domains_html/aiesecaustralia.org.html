AIESEC is a non-governmental not-for-profit organisation in consultative status with the United Nations Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC), affiliated with the UN DPI, member of ICMYO, and is recognised by UNESCO. AIESEC International is registered as a Foundation (Stichting), RSIN #807103895 in Rotterdam, The Netherlands.

We are AIESEC in Australia ltd, a youth leadership movement that is passionately driven by one cause: peace and fulfillment of humankind’s potential. We are registered in the official bodies of Australia under ABN 75 002 882 055 as well as a registered charity under Australian Charities and Non-for-profit commission. We comprise AIESEC in Australia ltd. and also the 12 local committees across Australia.
