Dysarts Service Center

Service Center & Wash Bay

Our full-service facility is a 16 bay shop with two lifts capable of handling all of your truck and trailer needs. We are a state-certified truck inspection facility and an authorized Carrier Transcold Dealer. We welcome RV services.

Dysarts services local fleets and independent tractor-trailers in need of repairs. In addition to trucking service, we welcome heavy equipment and machinery repairs. Our team of professionals brings years of experience and understands that each job is unique and requires individual attention.

Superior customer service and satisfaction is our priority.
