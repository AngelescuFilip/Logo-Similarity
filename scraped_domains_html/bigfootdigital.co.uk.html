Local SEO:

Local Barnsley SEO is a crucial aspect of search engine optimisation for businesses that serve a specific geographic area. By optimising your website and online presence for local search (in Barnsley or another local area), you can attract more targeted traffic from potential customers in your area, drive more foot traffic to your physical location, and ultimately increase your revenue.

Read More

Key Strategies for SEO Local Include:

  • Claiming and optimising your Google Business listing
  • Ensuring consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) information across online directories
  • Generating local citations and backlinks
  • Creating localised content that targets relevant keywords
  • Encouraging customer reviews on platforms like Google and Yelp

Local Technical SEO:

  • Ensure your website is mobile-friendly and responsive for local users searching on-the-go, as this is a crucial factor in SEO Barnsley
  • Optimise your website’s loading speed to provide a seamless user experience for local visitors, which can improve your search engine optimization efforts
  • Implement schema markup to help search engines understand your local business information, enhancing your visibility in local search results
  • Create a clear, user-friendly site structure that allows local visitors to easily find relevant information, a key aspect of effective SEO services
  • Set up Google My Business and ensure your NAP (Name, Address, Phone) information is consistent across all local directories, contributing to a strong local SEO strategy

National SEO:

National SEO focuses on improving your website’s visibility and rankings on a country-wide level. This is essential for businesses that operate on a larger scale or offer products and services nationwide. By consistently implementing best practices and staying up-to-date with the latest SEO trends, you can improve your website’s visibility and drive more organic traffic to your site.

Read More

To succeed with SEO national, you need to:

  • Conduct thorough keyword research to identify high-volume, relevant keywords
  • Create high-quality, informative content that targets these keywords
  • Build a strong backlink profile from reputable websites in your industry
  • Optimise your website’s technical elements, such as page speed and mobile-friendliness
  • Monitor and analyse your website’s performance using tools like Google Analytics

National Technical SEO:

  • Conduct a thorough website audit to identify and fix technical issues that may hinder your national search engine rankings
  • Optimise your website’s architecture and internal linking structure to ensure easy navigation and crawlability for search engine bots
  • Implement an SSL certificate to provide a secure browsing experience for visitors and improve your website’s credibility in search results
  • Utilise structured data markup to help search engines better understand and display your website’s content in national search results
  • Monitor and optimise your website’s page speed and performance to provide a positive user experience for visitors nationwide

ECommerce SEO:

ECommerce SEO is focused on optimising online stores to increase their visibility in search engine results and drive more organic traffic.

By investing in SEO ECommerce, online stores can improve their search engine rankings, attract more qualified traffic, and ultimately increase their sales and revenue.

Read More

Our ECommerce SEO strategy includes:

  • Optimising product pages with unique, keyword-rich titles and descriptions
  • Creating a clear, user-friendly site structure with intuitive navigation
  • Implementing schema markup to provide search engines with more context about your products
  • Generating high-quality backlinks from relevant websites and online directories
  • Optimising your website’s loading speed and mobile-friendliness to improve user experience

ECommerce Technical SEO:

  • Ensure your eCommerce platform is search engine-friendly and allows for easy crawling and indexing of product pages
  • Optimise your product pages with unique, keyword-rich URLs, titles, and descriptions to improve their visibility in search results
  • Implement a clear, hierarchical site structure with intuitive category and subcategory pages to help search engines understand your eCommerce store’s organisation
  • Use canonical tags to avoid duplicate content issues arising from product variations or pagination
  • Optimise your eCommerce store’s loading speed and performance to reduce cart abandonment and improve user experience for potential customers
