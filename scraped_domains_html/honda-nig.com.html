About Us

World no. 1 Motorcycle Brand

Honda Manufacturing (Nig.) Limited (“HMN”), is a subsidiary of Honda Motor Co. Japan and was incorporated in 1979.

Ever since, it has been the undisputed leader in the power products industry, manufacturing and marketing a range of Motorcycles, Portable Generators, Water Pumps and General Purpose Engine at its state-of-art manufacturing facilities at KM 6, Ota-Idiroko Road, Ota, Ogun State.

WHAT WE DO

#1 Motorcycle Brand in the World

Incorporated in 1979, Honda Manufacturing (Nig.) Limited (“HMN”) is a division of Honda Motor Co. Japan, and a top player in the power products sector in Nigeria. HMN assembles and sells a variety of motorcycles, portable generators, water pumps, and general-purpose engines at its cutting-edge production facilities at KM 6 on the Ota-Idiroko Road in Ota, Ogun State.
