Affresco products
The Fantasy collection is inspired by romantic dreams and fantasies. This is an improvisation on the theme of nature, where the creative imagination of our designers glorifies the world of ethnicity and folklore. Mythology and symbolism have become a source of inspiration for the collection. The design concept of the Line Art collection is a thin linear pattern, as the basis for creating new spatial solutions, and 160 interior background shades. The simplicity and conciseness of the line fill the interior with a unique style. The basis of the Emotion Art collection is the texture of natural materials, stone and textures created by artists: beautiful cuts of marble and onyx, the effect of wind and sea waves, where nature itself seems to have become the author. Affresco Wallpaper Часть i и ii is a collection of the most popular designs from the best collections of the factory: Vesna, Dream Forest, French Garden, Ethno, Botanika, Empire and Tsvetarium. New actual color combinations were added. Rio is the first Affresco catalog in a series of architectural historical wallpapers. Each image of the collection is an author's replica on the motives of the high French school of the late 18th – early 19th centuries. FLUID is a new collection of abstract designs from contemporary world artists. The collection is mainly devoted to drawings in the Fluid Art technique and artistic interpretations of various natural textures. Art Fabric Сollection - is a new approach to the design of individual interior fabrics, where the special beauty of the product is conveyed by the artistic drawing, and not by the variety of repeating elements. Atmosphere - the catalog of the most heartfelt stories to create author's interiors, immersing in the atmosphere of wild nature. Exclusive - is a revolutionary edition that challenges the usual idea of wallpaper design. Unique interiors with a detailed artistic drawing literally immerse in their fantasy world of beauty and harmony. Trend Art - the collection of modern author's wall-paper is devoted to the most popular trends of the present. The most winning colors in the interior are used in this collection, which include not only a soft range, but also bright juicy shades. Re-Space - new collection of unique designer wallpaper. The compositional structure of most designs has a repetitive pattern which allows you to create an infinite compositions in the interior. New Art - is an innovative collection of handmade wallpaper in the field of modern interior design. «Skazki Affresco» – is the most extensive catalog in the entire history of the factory. More than 90 fabulous masterpieces on the most popular themes for children's rooms. Fine Art is a new concept for creating a modern interior space from Affresco. Each design of the collection is able to endlessly excite the imagination, filling the interior with a mysterious atmosphere. Frescoes and photo wallpaper - your unique opprtunity to bring to life any interior ideas and fantasies. Convenient prefabricated design solution allowing to quickly change any interior.

WALL MASTERPIECES FROM AFFRESCO: FRESCOES, PHOTO WALLPAPER, WALLPAPER, PANELS

The company «Affresco» - is the manufacturer of wall murals, wallpaper, photo wallpaper and panels on the unique author's technology with the use of manual labor. All products have patents and quality certificates.

Production and sale of photo wallpaper, murals, panels

Frescoes - originally painted on damp plaster. They are decorated with the Sistine Chapel in Rome, the Palace of Knossos in Greece and other architectural monuments.

Affresco has created and patented a unique technology of digital fresco that reproduces all the features of this hand-painted painting. Such decor makes the interior a status and unique. With its help you can add a note of history or emphasize the modernity of the situation.

Frescoes and wallpaper: handmade

It is the largest library of author's images from professional artists and designers in our catalog of frescoes and photo wallpaper. "Affresco" offers more than 10 000 images of different styles and directions.

Seamless Wallpaper and panel:

"Affresco" is a manufacturer of hand-made wallpaper by its own patented technology. If you do not like the joints between the canvases, we will create a solid panel of the right size. The maximum size of a single canvas is 3,26х10,2 m. According to your order we will develop an individual sketch, we will select an image in the style of your interior, embody it in environmentally friendly materials and mount the most complex surface. "Affresco" company provides a full range of services, starting with the selection of images and finishing with the installation of frescos and photo wallpaper.

At the request of the client, we change the color range, sizes and proportions of the picture to the finished interior. If necessary, the artist will delete unnecessary objects or draw them. It is possible to replace the background, as well as color correction of individual fragments. The finished variant will be completely individual, and will correspond to all your wishes.

Pictures: individual creation

Our paintings are images on canvas with decorative plaster applied to the canvas. They are 90% manually created. You can order a miniature as well as a large or modular picture from any number of elements. Such posters look great in the interiors of different styles in combination with frescoes and wallpaper.

Online catalog of frescoes and wallpapers

Look in the "Products Affresco" section - it's easy to find an image that will make your interior unique. For convenience, the scenes are sorted by collections and styles.

Affresco products of the world level

Affresco is distinguished by:

  • A unique production technology of frescoes, wallpaper, panels, paintings. It is created and patented by us, there are no analogues in the world.
  • Professionalism of artists and designers. All specialists have a specialized art education and extensive experience in the field of design.
  • Individual production of frescoes. The products of "Affresco" are mostly made by hand, so frescoes and wallpaper with similar motives do not get the same.
  • Durability of products. Thanks to the protective coating developed by us, the canvases can be mounted in rooms with high humidity and even on the facades of buildings.
  • Protection against fakes. On all canvases (frescoes, photo wallpaper, panels, paintings) from Affresco there is a branded marking - a sign of quality, which the artist sets after checking the products. Each product has an individual number and branded packaging. Each tube is loaded with instructions for self-assembly.

On all products of our catalog official certificates of quality are received.
