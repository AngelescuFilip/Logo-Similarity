Sto is your partner for beautiful, sustainable, high performance building solutions.

Sto is the innovative world leader in the design and production of innovative building materials tailored to human needs. We are committed to developing next-generation building solutions that provide endless aesthetics in concert with our fully engineered wall systems. These include continuous insulation cladding systems, rainscreen systems, prefabrication solutions, air and moisture barriers, coatings, and restoration solutions. Sto has been the choice of leading architects, engineers and contractors for more than 40 years and our employees are advocates for technical mastery in pursuit of a sustainable built environment.   

At Sto, we help you realize your vision. 

Our Recent News

Sto Canada Ltd. Confirms Minimal Impact from Proposed U.S. and Canadian Counter-Tariffs, Highlights Strong German Heritage and Ongoing Commitment to Canadian Market

Sto Canada Ltd. announces that the anticipated U.S. tariffs and corresponding Canadian counter-tariffs, further postponed this week, will not have an immediate impact on its products or business operations.

 After a thorough review of the newly proposed measures, Sto Canada confirms that it does not sell into the U.S. market and that none of the categories targeted by Canada’s counter-tariffs affect the small number of products the company currently sources from the U.S.

Sto Canada is part of the global network of Sto SE & Co. KGaA companies, a global leader in the design of living space that is headquartered in Germany. This connection brings a rich heritage of German engineering and innovation—values that remain at the core of Sto’s operations worldwide.
