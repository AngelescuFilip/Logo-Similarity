Who We Are

 Overhead Door Company of Terre Haute was founded in 1929 as part of the W.J. Giffel Company. In 1996, Jim Stolt purchased the business and ran it with his son, Brian.  In July of 2018, Jim retired and now Brian and his team have taken over.

 

We are an IDEA accredited dealer and an A+ Better Business Bureau accredited company.  We are also members of various community organizations like the Greater Terre Haute Chamber of Commerce, Home Builders Association of Greater Terre Haute, and Wabash Valley Building Contractors Association.

What We Do

Overhead Door Company of Terre Haute has 11 employees and 5 service trucks on the road.  We serve 5 counties in Indiana and 5 counties in Illinois.  We work on residential and commercial doors and operators.  From a 6x6 golf cart door to a 40' hanger door we have a solution for almost any opening.
