FAQ

Why furnace maintenance is necessary?

An annual furnace tune-up is vital for maintaining the parts of the furnace. Regular maintenance will keep your furnace in proper working condition. Properly maintaining the furnace can reduce your energy bills, increase efficiency, reduce repairs, and keep your loved ones warm and safe.

Why do we need a professional for furnace service?

A professional with extensive experience, knowledge, and tools will provide the top-notch furnace repair/maintenance service. By calling a certified technician, you can rest assured that you hand over the dangerous task to the expert because fixing a faulty furnace by yourself can be hazardous for you and your family.

Why furnace inspection is necessary?

Many homeowners ignore furnace inspections, but they are foremost for the safety and efficiency of their heating systems. They not only prevent potential hazards but also extend the life of their heating systems and save money.

Are heat pumps noisy?

The noise of a heat pump depends on several factors, such as shape, size, model, and age. Choosing heat pumps designed for low noise is a great way to avoid issues with noisy heat pumps. If you are considering installing a heat pump and are also worried about its noise, consulting with a qualified and experienced installer is a good idea.

Will installing a heat pump increase electricity bills?

In short, yes, not because it is an electric appliance but because of its high efficiency. Heat pumps run on electricity, so your power bill will increase as the heat pump will heat your property as well.

Can I install a heat pump?

No, it is better to leave the job to a professional. Proper installation requires extensive experience, knowledge, and tools. A poor installation can lead to refrigerant leaks and electric fires, so it is good to hire a specialist.
