about us

Habitat for Humanity International began its work in Vietnam in 2001. Over the years, Habitat Vietnam has been delivering holistic approach to housing by supporting and empowering communities to take ownership in improving their living conditions and eliminate poverty housing. With our housing services, we have engaged many stakeholders to support families and community members to build strength, stability, and self-reliance by increasing the access to affordable, safe shelter through these thematic areas: inclusive housing; safe, smart, and resilient communities; housing affordability; and government-people engagement. Our project presence in Vietnam is in Thai Nguyen, Hoa Binh, Phu Tho, Thai Binh in the north; Ha Tinh and Quang Nam in the central; and Long An, Dong Thap and Tien Giang in the south.
