Introducing Habitat Home RePair

The Habitat Home RePair Program offers quality home repairs to homeowners in and around the HRM. When you book a repair with us, you’re not just fixing your own home—you’re also contributing to critical repairs for low-income households and seniors in the province.

It takes around 300 studs to frame a Habitat for Humanity home. The Stud-A-Thon is an easy and creative way to contribute to that effort!
