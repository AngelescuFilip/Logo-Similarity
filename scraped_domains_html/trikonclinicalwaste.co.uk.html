Your Clinical Waste Management Partner

Do you deal with clinical waste on a regular basis? You may need to collaborate with trusted professionals who can undertake clinical waste management. At Trikon, we are well-equipped to manage your clinical waste efficiently and safely. We are a fully accredited company offering clinical waste containers and personalised collection services for all kinds of health and self-care organisations. We will customise our service to suit your specific need, whether you are a general practitioner, dentist, care home or even a tattoo artist or beauty salon.

We undertake clinical waste management for healthcare facilities all across the UK. We have a team of highly-trained technicians who can safely and responsibly manage all kinds of clinical waste, including cytotoxic, anatomical, infectious, soiled or offensive, sanitary, laboratory and bagged clinical waste.

In terms of clinical waste management, we collect your waste daily, weekly or even fortnightly – whatever suits your requirement the best. Once collected, depending on the type of waste stream, we will treat it using the most sustainable methods. They include recycling materials where possible, diverting waste from a landfill and resorting to high-temperature incineration only when absolutely necessary.

So whatever your need, you can trust us to provide safe, sustainable and compliant clinical waste management.

Our ultra-low emission vans collect general waste from over 1500+ companies around the United Kingdom. We guarantee that we will deliver a far better solution than you currently have.

Top Rated Clinical Waste Management Company in the UK

Protecting individuals and communities while reducing the risk  involved with providing healthcare is at the forefront of our company’s mission. Our products and services are proven to minimise infection transfer through waste handling, and significantly improve the safety profile of all organisations that generate and handle healthcare waste.
