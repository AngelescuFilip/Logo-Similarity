CDVI Plastics

Founded in 1952, Alliance Plastique has acquired through out the years the know-how from the mould specialist and from the Plastic process specialist. Since the beginning, Alliance Plastique has been renewing its machinery assets for a better manufacturing perfomance but also to always be able to meet the customer's requirements.

Today the company has a very deep knowledge of the thermoplastic polymers. The company has acquired the capability of manufacturing from small size to large size lot production.

Here at CDVI, we have one clear aim – to become the installer’s choice. From a simple keypad and electric lock to a sophisticated online control system, we have a product range to cater for all your controlled access requirements
