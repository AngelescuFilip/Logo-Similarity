What To Do In Case Of A Water Damage Emergency

  • If possible, turn off the water at the source.
  • Turn off any electrical appliances that may be affected.
  • Remove any valuable items from the affected area.
  • Remove as many small items as you can, including small furniture and rugs.
  • If possible place foil under legs of larger pieces of furniture to reduce damage until restoration can begin.
  • DO NOT use television or other electrical items
  • DO NOT go into a room with a sagging ceiling
  • DO NOT attempt to disengage carpet yourself. Improper removal could cause additional damage to your carpet.
  • Call Duraclean right away. Time is of the essence.
