VEKA

VEKA Group is the world-wide market leader in uPVC System Windows Profiles. The company surpasses the high quality standards in Germany and Europe and is at the fore-front in the development of high-performance window profiles that can cope with increasingly strict environmental regulations.

VEKA MD82 System profiles have been certified as Passive House components by the independent Passive House Institute in Germany, fulfilling highest energy conservation requirements.

VEKA in Asia offers profiles for VEKA System Windows under two main product lines “SOFTLINE” and “MATRIX”.

About VEKA

VEKA Group is the world-wide market leader in uPVC System Windows Profiles. The company started its operations in 1969 as a manufacturer of shutters in Sendenhorst, Germany and has its own extrusion facilities in 12 countries (the United States, Russia, Poland, France, Spain, the United Kingdom, China, India, Canada, Mexico, Brazil and Chile) and representative offices in more than 40 countries across the world.
