Carpet and Furniture Cleaning for Scarborough, ME and Surrounding Communities

Thank you for considering Duraclean. We know that you have many choices in the cleaning field.

Since 1930 Duraclean has been the premier carpet cleaning, upholstery cleaning and restoration company. The current owner, Tom Dube, has been committed to offering the highest quality and best personal service in the local area for over 40 years.

It is this personal commitment and 'hands on approach' to service and quality that makes Duraclean stand out from the rest. This is why about 95% of business comes from repeat clients and their referrals. They know who to trust and where they can get the best value for their money spent.

Duraclean goes well beyond the results 'low priced' companies have to offer. There really is a Duraclean Difference!

"The bitterness of poor quality remains long after the sweetness of low price is forgotten." Benjamin Franklin.

With Duraclean's system, experience and knowledge, we feel confidant tackling everything from the grimiest of carpets to the most delicate of fine furniture fabrics.
It is important to note that Duraclean's cleaning solutions are environmentally friendly and nontoxic to people and pets. As 'green' cleaning chemistry evolves and improves, we are incorporating many more 'green' sourced products for our day-to-day operations.

We offer a variety of cleaning methods to meet your unique challenges. Duraclean uses specialized equipment and cleaning solutions to perform various cleaning services such as hot water extraction, hot foam absorption cleaning, low moisture encapsulation cleaning, and even drycleaning of draperies, furniture and carpets/rugs.

We are fully insured and offer a complete satisfaction guarantee.

If you would like to put Duraclean's experience to work for you, give us a call or email. Please have pertinent information available such as approximate size of rooms, number of pleats in your draperies, and perhaps what types of stains are present, so that we can determine an estimate over the phone. Unfortunately, we are extremely limited in time to do on-site estimating.
