We’re Duraclean, a full-service cleaning and restoration company based out of Belvidere, Illinois. Our primary services include water damage mitigation, mold remediation, and general cleaning. Duraclean specialists are available for both residential and commercial cleaning services. We offer 24/7 help to local businesses for when a disaster strikes.

Our company caters to homes and businesses across northern Illinois. We provide services to everyone in the Chicago metropolitan region. We also serve some parts of southern Wisconsin. Our staff are capable of traveling where we are needed and we respond promptly to any service call request regardless of location.

Your health is a top priority. We know it is our responsibility to efficiently clean and mediate any contamination issue. The results of our work will make a difference, better or worse. Our quality assurance measures are extensive. We do not let any of our clients down. In severe cases, like with mold damage mitigation, we make regular returning visits to ensure the dehumidification process is working and the issue hasn’t spread. We are proactive!

Services We Offer

Duraclean provides services to both residential and commercial clients.

For residential clients, the most common services we perform include hardwood floor, tile and grout, and carpet cleaning. We also provide air duct cleaning for your HVAC systems which is an incredible service. You will improve the quality of the air inside of your home and reduce health risks while also making your HVAC system work more efficiently. Aside from these services, we also offer dryer vent cleaning and upholstery cleaning to our residential clients.

For commercial clients, we also offer hardwood floor, tile and grout, and carpet cleaning services. Further, we provide water damage mitigation services any time a room floods or you notice sitting water. We can handle all types of water damage including sanitary, unsanitary, and black water damage. We are a full service water damage restoration company and can handle the worst of the worst. Additionally, we provide mold remediation services and ensure 100% efficiency to prevent any reappearances.

We’re Always Ready to Help!

Duraclean technicians are available at any time. Our commercial services are provided 24/7 because we know every second counts. Let us know if you have a crisis and need urgent help and we will arrange for our specialists to visit immediately.

We can work around your schedule. Call and book a cleaning service with Duraclean today!
