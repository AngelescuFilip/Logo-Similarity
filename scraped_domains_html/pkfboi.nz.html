Tēnā koutou

Paihia, te manawa o Pewhairangi. He kuwaha mo nga one me nga wahi ruku, te wahi whanau o te iwi.

‘Paihia the heart of the bay of Islands, known for its sandy beaches, gateway to the dive spots and the birthplace of our Nation’

 

 

PKF Bay of Islands has been in the business of providing accounting, business solutions and taxation advice in the Bay of Islands for the last 60 years.

Our experience demonstrates that every business is unique.  At PKF Bay of Islands we work for businesses and owners to tailor a unique solution in a timely and cost effective manner.

We help our clients to succeed in their business, and can confidently deal with property matters, investments, wealth creation and tax questions.

As a team of professionals with both real life business experience and academic qualifications, we invest heavily in making sure we keep ourselves up to date with the ever changing accounting, business and tax environment.

And, as an associate member of the PKF network, we have the backup of our New Zealand and International member firms.
