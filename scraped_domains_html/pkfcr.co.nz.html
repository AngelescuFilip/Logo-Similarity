Welcome to
PKF Corporate Recovery
& Insolvency (Auckland) Limited

 



 

 

PKF Corporate Recovery & Insolvency is a boutique insolvency firm providing specialist business recovery and insolvency services. 

Our firm is small with a strong reputation, and has had a significant number of high profile insolvency engagements in its time. We perform assignments of varying magnitudes within the Auckland region and nationally.

PKF Corporate Recovery is a certified chartered accounting practice and subject to the regulations of the New Zealand Institute of Chartered Accountants.

PKF Corporate Recovery are members of Chartered Accountants Australia and New Zealand (CAANZ) and Restructuring Insolvency & Turnaround Association of New Zealand (RITANZ).
