Toyota Batangas City, Inc. is a 9,898 square meter property located at Diversion Road, Barangay Balagtas, Batangas City. The total floor area of 4,436 square meters includes the Administration Building and two Service Shops. It was established last October 1997 under the dealership of Toyota Motor Philippines Corporation, which was incorporated on March 17, 1997.
