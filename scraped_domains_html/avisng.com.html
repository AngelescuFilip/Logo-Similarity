Rent A Car

We believe that car hire is more than getting from A to B. It’s about the journey and experiences you can have along the way and the cars you can explore in.

We provide services to both individuals and corporate bodies, locally and internationally. The rent-a-car segment of the business has direct links with all 10,000 AVIS rental locations, in approximately 175 countries around the world.

Fleet Management

This service involves organizations out-sourcing their transportation departments.

Out-source all your fleet of vehicles to us while we take care of your transportation department. We have 10 years of experience in fleet management and operating lease.

Chauffeur Service

We provide well trained, uniformed and professional chauffeurs who will meet your needs in the areas of defensive driving, car maintenance and good knowledge of road networks.

Haulage Service

Avis Haulage provides an efficient and effective distribution solution to national and multinational organizations with the essential daily haulage requirements that guarantee end-to-end distribution of goods. Our haulage service lease period ranges between 5-7 years.

Outbound reservations
