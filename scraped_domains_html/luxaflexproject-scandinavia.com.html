A customized solution

Customization in every window

Whatever product you choose from Luxaflex®, you will meet the result of more than sixty years of inspired design that offers innovative operating system and pure quality. We make each product individually by hand. That sets us apart from the others: our precise eye for detail in every tailor-made product.

"The difference between good and great is in the details."

We think that good design should also be beautiful, but our products reaches higher expectations than that. They offer excellent and flexible light regulation, insulation of both heat and cold, they save energy, protect your furniture against harmful UV rays, absorb sound and improve acoustics. We are committed to ensuring that a Luxaflex® choice is the right choice, for years to come.
