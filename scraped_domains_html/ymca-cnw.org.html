YMCA of CNW At A Glance

Infant, Toddler, Preschool & Pre-Kindergarten Programs
Our Early Learning Centers (ELCs) are NY State OCFS-licensed programs providing half-day, full-day, and extended-day care for children 6 weeks to 5 years old.

YMCA Teen Programs
The YMCA of Central & Northern Westchester uses a “build the whole person” model in our dynamic and unique teen programs. We have a variety of different programs to engage teens of all backgrounds & interests.
Click Here
YMCA Camp Combe
Located on the YMCA of CNW’s beautiful 100 acres facility in Putnam Valley, the  YMCA Camp Combe Day Camp serves campers ages 4-14.
Click Here
Volunteer At The Y
Across the US, more than 500,000 business leaders, community advocates, parents, teens, and individuals who want to give back & support their neighbors volunteer at the Y.

School Age Child Care
Where children in grades K-8 engage, learn, and achieve. Our Y’s School Age Care program extends your child’s learning day in a fun and meaningful way!

Work For The Y
When working at the Y you’ll find satisfaction in knowing that your contributions are helping to support young people, improve health & well-being, inspire positive action & so much more.
Click Here

 

By donating, you can ensure that every cent of your contribution goes towards giving children in your community the chance to participate in activities that encourage learning, development, and progress, such as arts, sports, STEM, and community service projects. In addition, your donation can assist in offering water safety education and nutritious food choices for children attending our programs. At the Y, every child is valued and supported.
