WHY CHOOSE US

Sri Lanka’s
most advanced construction and engineering solutions provider

Backed by the synergies and strengths of Hayleys PLC and as one of the leading construction and engineering companies in Sri Lanka, Advantis Engineering leverages its extensive construction and engineering experience acquired over the years to offer modern-day construction solutions to industries in Sri Lanka and beyond.

Team of Dedicated Construction Professionals

Advantis Engineering consists of construction professionals including civil and mechanical engineers, project managers and a plethora of construction experts with years of experience.

Dedicated to Our Clients

Advantis Engineering values itself in providing optimized construction solutions to its clients. A satisfied client and a successful construction project is of utmost importance to us.

Diversified Construction Portfolio

From Civil construction to steel buildings, Advantis engineering has the expertise and experience to handle all types of construction ventures.

Supporting Clients with Customised Solutions

As a client-focused building construction company, we understand that each project comes with unique challenges and requirements. That’s why we offer fully customised solutions that are tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients. Our comprehensive approach includes close collaboration with clients from the outset, ensuring that every detail of the project aligns with their vision and objectives.

How We Support Our Clients:
  • Collaborative Planning & Design: We work closely with our clients to develop detailed plans that meet both functional and aesthetic requirements. Our expert design team uses the latest technologies to ensure precision and creativity in every project.

 

  • Innovative Engineering Solutions: As one of the most advanced construction and engineering companies in Sri Lanka, we leverage cutting-edge technologies to optimise construction processes, improve efficiency, and ensure the highest level of safety on site.

 

  • Comprehensive Project Management: From start to finish, our dedicated project management team ensures smooth execution of every phase, delivering projects on time and within budget.
