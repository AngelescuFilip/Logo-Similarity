European Workforce Study

Our annual, cross-country European Workforce Study is a unique and comprehensive exploration of workforce culture and leadership across Europe. This groundbreaking research unveils the profound impact of these factors on productivity, innovation, and competitiveness. By basing our analysis on extensive research and direct employee feedback, we demonstrate our commitment to thought leadership. This study not only highlights key trends and insights but also reinforces our dedication to fostering a thriving and dynamic workforce on a global level.

Great Place To Work

What does it mean to be a great workplace? How do you measure trust at work? And how do you use the results to become one of the best employers in Belgium? Forty years of research and experience have enabled us to formulate the answers. And, above all, develop the tools you need to assess and improve your employee experience according to the global standard of greatness.

So you can inspire your people to achieve their true potential. Drive performance to all-new heights. And offer your employees the workplace they deserve. It’s time to become a Great Place To Work®.
