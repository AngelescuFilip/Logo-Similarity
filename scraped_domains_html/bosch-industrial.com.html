    Heat generation without emissions? The ELHB electrifies yours!

    Improve your carbon footprint with the new ELHB electric boiler for heating and hot water. The ELHB uses power from renewable energy sources highly efficiently and generates up to 5.5 MW heat in a climate-neutral way. 100% electric, 0% flue gas emissions.
