About us

 UNITECNICA was founded in 1992 and provides technically excellent engineering services across a broad range of industrial markets, including the upstream, midstream and downstream oil & gas, power & process, pulp and paper, chemistry, mining, nuclear, fertilizers, sugar and alcohol.
