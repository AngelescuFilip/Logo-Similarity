Is Your Child Sick?

Welcome to Advocare Newborn Network

The Advocare Newborn Network is a group of doctors, nurse practitioners, and lactation consultants dedicated to supporting healthy newborns and their families in the first days and weeks of life. Our experienced medical providers take the time to educate, encourage, and empower new families to become competent, joyful caregivers of their newborn. We want families to feel successful in navigating the social, emotional and nutritional challenges of early infancy.


We believe in integrating the best practices of care as determined by: the American Academy of Pediatrics, the American College of Obstetrics and Gynecology, the World Health Organization, and the Academy of Breastfeeding Medicine, to ensure a happy, healthy transition for the family and newborn baby.
