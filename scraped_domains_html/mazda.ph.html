WHY MAZDA

A HERITAGE BORN OF PASSION

Rooted in the heart of Japan's industrial epicenter, Mazda stood apart from convention by charting its own path. By believing in ourselves amidst challenges, we cultivated a culture of innovation that is alive to this day. Every Mazda vehicle is a product of our passion to excel.

LEARN MORE

DESIGNED TO INSPIRE

Every shape we mold, every line we carve is a product of passion. Our craftsmen's dedication to create design that moves people and elicits joy is key to making you want to drive our cars even more. Color, light, shadow and obsession to details are pathways to elevate your experience in every Mazda.

LEARN MORE

THE FUTURE OF DRIVING TODAY

Our dedication towards a world where man, machine and society coexist in harmony is unwavering. We are committed to harness the full efficiency of every technology at its disposal. Mazda Skyactiv Technology is at the forefront of providing solutions to environmental issues facing the planet.

LEARN MORE

A HUMAN-CENTRIC APPROACH

The bond betweeen driver and car is essential to driving fulfillment. That is why Mazda craftsmen pour their attention and dedication to every Mazda interior. The seamless harmony of elegance and functionality is key to achieving oneness in every drive.

LEARN MORE

MAZDA HYBRID

An innovative evolution of hybrid car technology, Mazda Hybrid harnesses every bit of energy normally wasted during braking so the engine and integrated electric motor can work in harmony to simultaneously propel the vehicle.

LEARN MORE

AUTOEXE

Founded by former Mazda works driver Yojiro Terada, AutoExe is Mazdas specialist tuning company and performance parts manufacturer dedicated to creating a holistic driving experience that brings out the full potential of Mazdas Jinba-ittai philosophy, emphasizing the unity between car and driver.

Learn More

AWARD-WINNING MASTERPIECE

The Mazda CX-30 was named Top Gear Philippines 2020 Car of the Year, Editors Choice. The Mazda3 Sportback also received the accolade of Hatchback of the Year, while the Mazda BT-50 Pangolin Edition was named Love the Planet Awardee.

Learn More

A CENTURY OF INNOVATION

Born in Hiroshima, Japan, and infused with the Challenger Spirit. Mazda continues to defy convention by bravely facing any challenge with grit and determination. We celebrate driving through 100 Years of engineering and design excellence. Every vehicle we make is a reflection of who we are and what we stand for.

AWAKENING THE SENSES

From the moment you start a Mazda, you will feel the instant connection and bond with the car. This is Jinba Ittai. Strengthening this bond is our goal. This direct and instantaneous feeling of input to movement is what sets our cars apart from the rest.
