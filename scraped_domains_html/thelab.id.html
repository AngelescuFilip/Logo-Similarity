Awards & Accreditations

The Best Coding School for your Child

Experiential Learning
The Lab provides opportunities to encourage active involvement and meaningful interactions using robots designed to engage and develop the senses and intellectual capability of the student.
Problem-based Learning
We focus on the student’s reasoning and reflection to construct their own learning. As such, we work with various stakeholders to provide real-world simulations and environments for students to discover the world of technology.
Purposeful Exploration
Activities are student centric to help them become independent learners, leading to greater self-confidence, and the ability to face new challenges. We combine experimentation and imagination to ignite curiosity and creativity.
Personalised and Self-Directed Learning
Learning is personal and individual. Each child learns differently and at a varying pace. We recognize and embrace those differences, providing opportunities for children to learn using a variety of strategies and contexts.
