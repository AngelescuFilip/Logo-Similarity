Allianz in Singapore

In November 1998, Allianz set up its Asian regional office in Singapore. The move demonstrates Allianzs firm commitment to better serve the fast-growing Asian markets with a combination of global strength and local expertise.

With a desire to deliver best-in-class solutions to customers, and bringing a strong brand that stands for quality, trust and resilience one step closer to Singapore, a composite insurance entity, Allianz Insurance Singapore Pte. Ltd. has now been set up on 08 June 2020 to better serve the local retail, SME and mid-corp markets via various channels including agents, bancassurance, brokers, digital platforms, financial advisers, and partnerships.

Allianz is the No. 1 insurance brand again in 2024*.
Allianz Insurance Singapore has been assigned an 'A+' rating from Standard & Poors in 2024.

*Interbrand Best Global Ranking
