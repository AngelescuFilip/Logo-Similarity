Campus & Facilities

At LVS Oxford, our pupils learn a range of academic and vocational skills in a vibrant and stimulating environment. Our school is located on a converted priory, providing a purpose-designed work environment, all set within a tranquil and relaxing green campus on the outskirts of Oxford.

How To Apply

We strongly believe that referrals and admissions to the school should be a partnership building process with parents, the placing authority and the school, designed to ensure that the school can meet the needs of the prospective learner.

READ MORE

Wellbeing

Learning for wellbeing provides pupils with the skills and knowledge to be physically and mentally healthy and have the stamina to sustain work.

We support the learning process by focusing on the emotional wellbeing and physical health of our pupils.

READ MORE

Curriculum

At LVS Oxford, we have Academic and ‘Learning for Life’ pathways which are designed to help our students develop their aspirations and achieve the next step toward their desired destination. To support this, we help our students build core traits that promote their personal wellbeing and help them develop lifelong learning skills.

READ MORE

Therapies

At LVS Oxford, we support our pupils to develop calming strategies suited to the individual to help deal with anxiety. Our specialist staff are key members of our school, there to support pupils on a daily basis. We have a dedicated speech and language room and a separate therapy room.

Our therapy is integrated across the curriculum, with pupils able to access specialist (1:1), targeted (group work) and universal provision (blended across the full curriculum).

READ MORE

Moving On

The focus of our education is to improve the outcome possibilities for our pupils. We aim to prepare young people with autism for life in the workplace so that when they leave us they have the skills and knowledge to be able to get a job and sustain employment.
