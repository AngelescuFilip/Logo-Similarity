DAIKIN SUSTAINABLY REPORT 2024

Daikin Industries, Ltd. has released its English edition of Sustainability Report 2024. This report summarizes Daikin Group's ESG information including its basic approach to contributing to a sustainable society through its business, fiscal 2023 activities and results, and plans for the future. The 2024 edition describes Daikin's attitude in achieving both business growth and the realization of a sustainable society, centered on the key themes in the three-year plan for the latter half of Fusion 25 strategic management plan formulated in fiscal 2023.
