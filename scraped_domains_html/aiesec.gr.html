WHO ARE WE?

AIESEC is the world’s largest youth-run organization. Since 1948 globally and since 1956 in Greece, we have been developing leadership skills in young people by providing them with practical experiences in environments that are both challenging & supportive. We work alongside Universities, companies and youth to make our vision reality!

AIESEC

AIESEC is the largest network managed and composed exclusively of individuals aged 18-30, with a presence in more than 120 countries and regions. Its purpose is to develop leadership skills in young people through intercultural exchanges. These exchanges can be either voluntary or practical training and aim at the personal and professional development of youth while simultaneously creating a positive impact on society. Since 2015, AIESEC has been collaborating with the UN to achieve Sustainable Development Goals.

AIESEC in Greece has a physical presence in over 7 universities throughout Greece and is continuously expanding. In the last 2 years alone, 2000 young people have participated in our programs, and today, we have over 300 active members in our organization.
