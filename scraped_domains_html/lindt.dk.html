NEW! Discover Lindt Dubai Style Chocolate

Indulge in the luxurious taste of Lindt Dubai Style Chocolate, a unique creation by our expert Lindt Maîtres Chocolatiers. This exceptional chocolate bar features a rich filling made with 45% pistachios, offering a delightful nutty flavor that will impress.

 

Crafted with the finest ingredients, Lindt Dubai Style Chocolate combines smooth Lindt milk chocolate with a creamy pistachio paste and crunchy Kadayif, creating a harmonious blend of textures and flavors. Each bite delivers an unforgettable experience, making it a perfect treat for chocolate lovers.

Limited Edition

Lindt Dubai Style Chocolate

Lindt Dubai Style Chocolate is an exclusive chocolate bar made of the finest milk chocolate, enclosing an exceptionally soft and crispy filling with 45% pistachios and crispy pieces of kadaifi.

Each ingredient is carefully selected and skillfully combined by Lindt Master Chocolatiers to create a taste experience that is both sophisticated and memorable.
