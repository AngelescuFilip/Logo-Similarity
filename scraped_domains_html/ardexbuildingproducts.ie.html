About ARDEX Building Products Ireland Limited

ARDEX Building Products Ireland Limited is one of the leading suppliers of adhesives, grouts, flooring preparation products and specialist industrial floor coatings to the Irish flooring and tiling market. With core brands including ARDEX, BAL and Dunlop, the company provides support including technical advice, site surveys and extensive training through its own training centre in Dublin.

The ARDEX Group vision is: “To be one of the world’s leading solution providers of high performance special building materials”.
