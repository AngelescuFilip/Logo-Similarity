Benefits Of Using eBay Global Shipping Services

HASSLE - FREE REGISTRATION

Complete your registration with zero registration charges and with no requirement of a minimum deposit

COMPETITIVE PRICING

Enjoy the choice of multiple logistics partners along with option to compare rates across various destinations.

SELLER PROTECTION

Get protected for account defects due to Late Shipments Rate (LSR), Item Not Received Disputes (INR) and neutral/negative feedback

DEDICATED SUPPORT FROM LSP

Get help through the dedicated support by each Logistics partner and our in house “Resolution Center” team for shipping related queries

WIDE NETWORK DISTRIBUTION

Ship across the borders through our various Logistic Service Partners and choose from multiple services such as Priority, Economy & Postal

AUTOMATED SHIPPING UPDATES

Ease of accessibility and usage via automation of tracking upload of eBay orders, generating shipping labels and summary reports.
