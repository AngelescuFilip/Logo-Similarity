Partnership - Franchise

Franchise is a form of association of owners in a large team with the ability to use the infrastructure, promotion of the brand and work in team of professionals, minimizing risks, moving directly to a high level immediately after opening.Franchising provides many opportunities for network development.

The world’s largest companies, such as McDonalds, KFC, and Radisson Blue, have been using franchising for decades.Without franchising today, they would not become global networks that create standards in entire industries. For example, more than 80% of McDonald’s restaurants have a franchise.

Franchising, as a system, has provided itself as a way to quickly promote your business. According to objective statistic, using this system of cooperation allows you: to overcome the most difficult first years company’s formation and continue to work with high profits, protect your money and nerves in creating your own brand, compensate for the lack of resources for full competition with large networks.

For comparison: most of the newly created small companies in the service sector do not survive even up to 2 years. That is why our company has focused on constructing, first of all, an affiliate network through the development of effective franchising.We invite you to become part of the team of super – professionals!

AVIA gas station network

Our goal goes far beyond the corporate needs, we aspire to create a business – community of like-minded people and leaders in the field of distribution of products of the fuel and energy industry.

The network of “AVIA”  is a modern technological complex for selling of fuel products and customer serviceces. The network consists of a large number of core business units of the network, namely the gas station.

The gas station “AVIA” – as a product, consists of a number of elements that form a single system.

The main structural elements include:

Refueling complex for Petroleum products, Liquefied gas (LPG), Charging stations for electric cars, Parking, Shop, Cafe, Places for rest, Lavatory.

Services provided:

  • distribution of petroleum products
  • liquefied gas
  • car charging
  • smelling coffee and catering
  • sale of products, grocery and household goods.

Esenco Cafe Network

In the 21st century, the gas station is not only the place where you can buy gasoline or gas for your car. These filling stations have turned into complete centers where every driver has the opportunity to have a rest, to drink a fragrant coffee, to take a meal, as well as to make the necessary purchases in the store .

Each of our gas stations, has a full network cafe. Cafe Escenco is our brand name, which has its mission and philosophy. In our cafe there is always only delicious and fragrant coffee, a high level of service, tasty and healthy food, and everything you need for a good rest.

What does our cafe offer:

Coffee drinks, a large selection of dishes from small snacks to full-fledged hot meals, and groceries.

Additional services:

  • WIFI
  • places to rest
  • cozy and comfortable places
  • safety and assistance as needed.
