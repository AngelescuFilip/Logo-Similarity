    Sustainability with Style

    Our stylish, eco-friendly rugs are crafted from recycled materials. With a single-material construction for effortless recycling, each rug offers you the perfect blend of fashion and environmental consciousness.

    Machine washable

    Keeping your Ecorug looking brand new is hassle-free and one of the best ways to ensure it has a long life. That’s why we’ve made every Ecorug machine washable and provided the perfect care instructions for your new rug.

    Take Care program

    When your Ecorug has reached the end of its time in your home, returning it is easy. Simply scan the QR code to arrange a free return. Through our Take Care program, we can work together to give Ecorugs a circular life.

    Recycle

    Once your Ecorug returns to us, we recycle it by breaking down all the fibers to create new recycled Ecorugs. This process ensures a fully circular and sustainable lifecycle, allowing us to continually produce high-quality, eco-friendly rugs while minimizing waste.
