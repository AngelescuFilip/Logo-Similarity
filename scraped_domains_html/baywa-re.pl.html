Renewable energy with sustainable values


Benefit from BayWa r.e.’s many years of international experience in solar and wind energy projects. We deliver renewable energy solutions worldwide and we are a leading supplier to the international solar distribution market. We nurture long-term relationships with our customers, with our company values of and high-quality standards at their heart. Let’s take a stand against climate change and build a renewable future together.
