PKF UAE – Leading Auditors, Business Consultants, Accountants & Tax service providers in Dubai and Abu Dhabi.

We deliver top-notch audit, outsourced accounting & payroll, consulting and tax services with a focus on client-focused, business-centric solutions. Organisations trust our team of professional advisers for high-value advice grounded on global expertise and local insight. The PKF family includes over 500 offices, 214-member firms operating in 150 countries across five regions and employing over 23,000 professionals.

PKF UAE currently has 8 partners with over 250+ staff members respectively specialising in audit and assurance, entry strategy, company formation, risk advisory, tax consulting & compliance, outsourced accounting & payroll, consulting, business advisory and corporate finance.

Are you looking for an audit, accounting, payroll, tax advisory and consulting firm in Dubai and Abu Dhabi?

PKF UAE adds value through its professional audit, accounting, consulting and tax services by evaluating how things are, and then asking, how can they be made better?

Global Presence. Local Expertise

Established in the UAE in 1976.
Registered with the (DFSA) Dubai Financial Services Authority and (ADGM) Abu Dhabi Global Market.

Why Choose PKF?

With a proud legacy of over 45 years in the UAE and a team of 250 skilled professionals, PKF UAE combines local expertise with a deep understanding of the dynamic Middle Eastern market. This unparalleled experience allows us to deliver tailored solutions that empower businesses to navigate challenges and seize opportunities with confidence.

At PKF UAE, our approach is grounded in practicality and client-focused service. Every assignment is led by an engagement partner or director, ensuring strategic oversight and a personalized touch. Our team is committed to providing dependable and timely responses, addressing your unique business needs with agility and precision.

We pride ourselves on crafting bespoke solutions that align with your goals and aspirations. Whether you're seeking advice to streamline operations, expand your footprint, or fortify your financial strategies, PKF UAE is your trusted partner, dedicated to driving your success at every step.
