Why Choose Culligan

30 Day Money Back Guarantee​

At Culligan of Hastings we go to great lengths to test your water, inspect your plumbing, and ask questions about your family’s water needs to make sure you’re happy with your Culligan water from day one. However, if we ever make a mistake, let us know within 30 days and we’ll be glad to correct it or refund your money.​

Trusted Culligan Man Service​

Unlike big box stores and fly-by-night water companies, Culligan has put its customers first since 1938 by taking care of them long after the sale. Keeping you happy for the long haul is what we’re all about and how we’ll continue to build our reputation for decades to come.​

We’re Local & Family Owned​

We’ve had the opportunity to serve West Michigan since 1984 and help provide outstanding products and services to our customers. We have the technology and research of a global corporation backing us, however, at our heart, we’re a small mom and pop shop here to serve you. We’re proud to help our team members be involved in our communities.​
