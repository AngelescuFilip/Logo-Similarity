The biggest network in Europe

Pirtek entered the European marketplace in 1988 and has grown to become Europe’s number one provider of on-site hydraulic hose replacement services. Pirtek Europe has more than 200 service centres and a fleet of approximately 800 mobile service vehicles operating across eight countries – Austria, Belgium, France, Germany, Ireland, Netherlands, Sweden and the UK. Pirtek’s mission is to provide the fastest and most convenient service available, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. It offers a comprehensive range of products and services across many market sectors.

Over 30 years of expertise

We play a leading role when it comes to Health & Safety, technician training and environmental practices, helping drive the entire industry forward.
