For over 45 years, ElderHouse Adult Day Center, an accredited not-for-profit organization located in Fairfield County, has remained proudly committed to supporting older adults and their family caregivers. To help care for the ones you love, ElderHouse offers two programs.


Spend the Day with Us
Through our Adult Day Center, ElderHouse offers opportunities for social engagement, enjoyable activities, memory care, nursing supervision, and personal attention at an affordable all-inclusive rate. Families benefit from reliable, high-quality care for their loved ones with regularly scheduled days off from their caregiving duties; and the peace of mind knowing their loved ones are remaining active in a safe compassionate setting.  Enroll today! Call us for a complimentary day visit at (203) 847-1998.

Stay Home and Get Paid
ElderHouse is a provider of the State of Connecticut’s Adult Family Living Program. Caregivers who choose to care for their loved ones at home, receive expert advice on managing daily tasks and a monthly tax-free stipend. Call us to see if you qualify.

ElderHouse serves aging adults in Darien, New Canaan, Norwalk, Stamford, Weston, Westport, and Wilton.
