Families On Challenge

Join your friends and family and race to the finish through a three-mile challenge course at the A.E. Finley YMCA this spring. The YMCA Families On Challenge combines fun obstacles with challenges that your whole family can enjoy.

Golf Marathon

Play 100 holes of golf for the YMCA's Annual Campaign. Three YMCAs, Taylor, Kraft and Northwest Cary, are teaming up for this event. Golfers and volunteers welcome!

Learn More

Delta Swim for Life

Swim for Life offers free swim lessons for children and adults based on income qualifications. The program is funded through the YMCA's Annual Campaign.

 

We rely on volunteers to help teach water safety and help with check-in. Volunteers need to be comfortable in the water and have some swimming experience.
