PKF Thailand

We are physically present in all the major Thai economic centers.

Globally PKF operates in over 150 countries from over 400 locations.

This makes PKF Thailand an ideal partner for ambitious Thai businesses with overseas expansion plans, as well as for foreign companies already present in or looking to enter the Thai market.

As an SEC-licenced firm we are the auditor of a number of Thai listed companies.

Our Expertise

Whether you are looking to set up a BOI-registered business or seeking help with your tax and accounting, PKF Thailand is committed to helping you succeed.
