About alchemia-nova

alchemia-nova is a research and innovation institute dedicated to spreading nature-based solutions and circular economy principles to transition towards climate-smart and sustainable societies. We conduct research and provide a range of services and products that enable our partners to take a holistic approach in sectors such as water management, resilient cities and infrastructure, agriculture and food, and industrial production. Our team continues to expand our collective knowledge by participating in various research and innovation projects. We also focus on developing sustainable practices and technologies through collaboration with global partners. Our aim is to drive positive change and create resilient and circular communities, businesses, and regions.

Research & Consulting

We deliver a wide range of research and consulting services that promote sustainability, resilience, and innovation across multiple sectors. Our expertise covers the circular bioeconomy, climate change adaptation, nature restoration and pollution control, and capacity building in these fields. Our comprehensive support includes policy assistance, facilitation of stakeholder engagement processes, technical guidance, engineering design, installation and monitoring of demonstration sites, and specialized maintenance. We work with cities, communities, regional and national governments, businesses, and educational institutions to adopt sustainable practices and achieve environmental, economic, and social benefits.

Our services
  • Circular Bioeconomy
    Supporting enterprises and regions in transforming linear waste generating systems and supply chains into sustainable resource-oriented value cycles.
  • Climate Change Adaptation
    We help build climate resilience through nature-based solutions and circular water management.
  • Nature Restoration and Pollution Control
    Climate change adaptation measures, nature-based solutions, and water management.
  • Capacity Building
    Transforming agricultural bio-waste into valuable bio-products, addressing unemployment and bio-waste management issues through capacity building in agro-entrepreneurship.

Circular economy
in Austria

kreislaufwirtschaft.at provides a platform for citizens, entrepreneurs, and decision-makers. It offers innovative ideas and solutions for an active circular economy in Austria to facilitate dynamic exchange and implementation of the basic principles of the circular economy. Kreislaufwirtschaft.at provides accessible knowledge, tools, and implementation strategies for transforming linear waste streams into nutrient cycles in cities.

Kreislaufwirtschaft.at is managed by alchemia-nova as part of the DIRECT HUBS project, funded by the Climate and Energy Fund and carried out as part of the ‘energy transition 2050’ program. Within the framework of the DIRECT HUBS project, a concept for local innovation and transformation hubs in cities is being developed, where citizens participate in shaping, innovators have a platform, and solutions are developed in dynamic exchange within the community.
