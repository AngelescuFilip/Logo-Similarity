Daikin Malaysia Sdn. Bhd. (Myanmar Branch) is a subsidiary of Daikin Industries Ltd. Established in 1924 with headquarters in Osaka, Japan, Daikin began operations as a manufacturer of aircraft radiator tubes. Today, with over 199 subsidiaries and 38,000 employees worldwide, the Daikin Group maintains that tradition of excellence as the world’s leading manufacturer of unitary and applied air conditioning equipment and systems for residential, commercial, and industrial applications.
Since 1st June 2014, Daikin Air-conditioning (S) Pte. Ltd. (Myanmar Branch) has started branch office in Hledan Center to conduct sales and service support to Myanmar dealers and consumers. In April 2016, Daikin Myanmar Branch has continued to reach greater heights by becoming one of the subsidiaries of Daikin Malaysia Sdn. Bhd., one of the leading manufacturers of HVAC products (heating, ventilation and air-conditioning) and also a member of the Daikin group Japan.
