Welcome to
PKF JND, P.C.

 

PKF JND, P.C. has trusted accountants and business advisers that are ready to provide the services you are looking for. We are a full service accounting firm providing audit, tax and consulting services. With offices in both Boston and Quincy, Massachusetts, we are conveniently located to provide close contact and rapid response to our clients, while having the national and international resources to draw on through our membership in PKF International.

The difference among large accounting and consulting firms are not to be found in the services they offer. Rather, these differences lie with people who compose each firm.  We believe that the quality of service depends on these people which is why we dedicate the necessary resources to development and training. We select our professionals based on their specific experience, dedication to client service and commitment to forming a partnership with each client. We stress the importance of communication and working closely as a team. If you are interested in joining our team we would like to hear from you.
