Quality Assurance in Testing and Teaching with ISLPR®

We oversee and implement strict quality assurance for the use of the

ISLPR® in teaching and testing.

Who is the IGAC?

The ISLPR Global Accreditation Company’s (IGAC) purpose is to provide quality assurance to all end-users that accept the ISLPR® for high-stakes certification by accrediting testers, teachers and tutors who administer the ISLPR® and quality assuring all test results. It is in the interest of candidates, testers and end-users of test results to ensure that any results received for university entry purposes, vocational (including teacher) registration or any other purpose have been quality assured.

To be relevant to a learner’s real-life language needs, teaching and testing must focus on a candidate’s real-life practical language skills. Our vision is to provide ESL speakers in Australia, New Zealand and beyond, an alternative to the generic way of teaching and testing English.

  • Australian Owned & Operated – The ISLPR® and PALT™ are wholly Australian produced and owned.

  • Global focus – we are expanding our reach globally with the development of an Online English Test.

  • Domestic centres – we have offices in Brisbane & Melbourne and are expanding our permanent presence across Australia and New Zealand in 2021.

QUALITY ASSURANCE

Quality assurance is built into all major international tests; ISLPR® is no different and ISLPR® tests are subject to strict quality assurance procedures. Test results that have been quality assured and accredited by IGAC will carry  a stamp along with the statement:

“These results have been accredited by the ISLPR® Global Accreditation Company”.

Institutions accepting the ISLPR® are recommended to show the ISLPR® on their list of accepted tests in this way: ISLPR® (IGAC accredited) 

ACCREDITATION

TESTER ACCREDITATION

Every year the IGAC offers a training program conducted by a qualified specialist (under the authority of the ISLPR® Global Accreditation Company). The purpose of our training program is to recruit new testers who will gain employment at one of our accredited testing centres.

From the introductory training programme you will gain knowledge in:

  • The theoretical background to the ISLPR®
  • The methodology of ISLPR® language proficiency testing
  • The ISLPR® scale
  • How to select testing materials

For more information about our recruitment process, visit our tester accreditation page here.

TEACHER ACCREDITATION

The IGAC offers a training program conducted by a qualified specialist (under the authority of the ISLPR® Global Accreditation Company). The purpose of our training program is to recruit new teacher who will gain employment at one of our accredited testing centres as an English teacher/tutor.

From the introductory training programme you will gain knowledge in:

  • The theoretical background to the ISLPR®
  • Proficiency Applications in Language Teaching (PALT)
  • The ISLPR® scale

For more information about using the ISLPR in teaching and accreditation for language institutions, visit our accreditation page here.

LANGUAGE CENTRE ACCREDITATION

An ISLPR® Global Accredited Language Centre is an organisation that makes use of and references the ISLPR® in their teaching, tutoring or courses (including in course development, streaming and assessment). The IGAC offers an accreditation program conducted by a qualified specialist (under the authority of the IGAC). Accreditation ensures the organisation is referencing the ISLPR® correctly as well as authorising the use of the ISLPR® trademark in course materials and marketing materials.

From the introductory training programme you will gain knowledge in:

  • The theoretical background of the ISLPR®
  • Proficiency Applications in Language Teaching (PALT)
  • The ISLPR® scale

For more information about accreditation for language institutions, visit our accreditation page here.

TEST CENTRE ACCREDITATION

An ISLPR® Global Accredited Test Centre is an organisation that has received a licence from the IGAC to produce accredited ISLPR® tests. All tests are quality assured by the IGAC through a rigorous moderation process and all certificates are issued by the IGAC.

To apply for a licence to establish an ISLPR test centre, click here.
