About Würth Group

  • The Würth Group is world market leader in its core business, the sale of assembly and fastening materials. It currently consists of over 400 companies in more than 80 countries and has approximately 77,000 employees on its payroll. Approximately 33,000 of these are permanently employed sales representatives. According to the annual financial statement, the Würth Group generated total sales of EUR 14.3 billion in the business year 2019.





About Würth Group

The Würth Group is world market leader in its core business, the sale of assembly and fastening materials.
