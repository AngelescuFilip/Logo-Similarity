Serving the Needs of Low Income Families and Seniors

The properties highlighted on this website are managed by The John Stewart Company for The Santa Clara County Housing Authority (SCCHA). They are primarily tax credits and all housing is independent living, a few properties offer project based Section 8 rental assistance. You may be a senior to reside in family housing, but all senior housing has minimum age restrictions.  Refer to property profile for property specific age restrictions at senior sites.

Please apply online to the property that is accepting applications. Waitlist Interest List Applications will only be accepted online for the designated period of time stated, and will be put into a lottery to be placed on the waiting list for that specific property. All other applications will be accepted on-going until noted “WAITLIST IS CLOSED” for any specific property.

Should you require a Reasonable Accommodation in completing any application, please contact the property directly. English is the official language version of the applications and all documents; translation is only provided as a convenience.

Smoking Policy

All Santa Clara County Housing Authority properties are non-smoking and the non-smoking policy is strictly enforced. No person is allowed to smoke on the property or in their unit.

Fair Housing

We do business in accordance with Federal Fair Housing Law. It is illegal to discriminate against any person because of Race, Color, Religion, Sex, Handicap, Sexual Orientation, Gender Identity, Familial Status or National Origin.
