About AVIS Malawi

Avis Malawi has been operating in the country for more than 40 years now and has a fleet of over 200 vehicles of different sizes and models, and within our fleet we have new models and good quality vehicles. This is because, we adhere to the AVIS worldwide standard safety requirements. All 4x4 vehicles are fitted with tracking devices as an optional services. Occasionally, most of our vehicles comes with full tank fuel, air-condition, radio, CD, triangles and fire extinguisher. Our fleet is categorised into various groups as per size and engine capacity. Also, the rental prices we offer, are based on car groups and not engine capacity. Avis has different services that are offered, like for instance: Chauffeur Drive, Self - Drive and Point to Point.

  • Chauffer Drive

    This is a service where we provide trained chauffeurs as per the Avis International Policies ,qualifications and vast experience to all our clients

  • Self Drive

    This is a service where the client opts to drive themselves.

  • Point to Point

    This is a service where we pick clients from one point and drop them at one point either from airports to hotels or homes to weddings or meeting places.. .
