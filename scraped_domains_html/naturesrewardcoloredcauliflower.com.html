Welcome to Nature's Reward Colored Cauliflower

At Nature’s Reward, we are dedicated to educating our community about the vibrant and nutritious world of colored cauliflower. Our mission is to spread awareness about the health benefits and culinary versatility of these beautiful vegetables. Join us on a journey of discovery and delight as we explore the wonders of nature’s colorful bounty.

OUR COLORS

Orange Cauliflower

Orange cauliflower, also called cheddar cauliflower, is known for its bright color and high beta-carotene content. This variety has a mild, creamy flavor and provides a unique twist to traditional cauliflower dishes.

Purple Cauliflower

Purple cauliflower boasts a vibrant hue and a slightly sweeter, nuttier flavor than white cauliflower. Its striking color comes from natural anthocyanins, which are powerful antioxidants. It’s perfect for adding visual appeal and health benefits to your dishes.

Green Cauliflower

Green cauliflower, also known as broccoflower, has a mild, slightly sweet taste with a hint of broccoli flavor. This variety is rich in vitamin C and fiber, making it a nutritious addition to salads, stir-fries, and more.

FIND RECIPES

Romanesco Cauliflower

Romanesco cauliflower, with its stunning fractal florets and vibrant green color, offers a mild, nutty flavor. It’s not only visually captivating but also rich in vitamins C and K. This unique vegetable adds a crunchy texture and an artistic touch to any dish.
