Welcome at TTS (Transport Trade Services) GmbH., Austria

Today, our group of companies is one of the largest and most entrusted river shipping companies in Europe.

Through our sister companies we are owning and operating a fleet of more than 50 pushers, over 400 covered and open top barges as well as a fleet of tank barges.

Our services are offered from the Black Sea, via the Danube, Main and Rhine to the North Sea ports of Amsterdam, Rotterdam, Antwerp, and Ghent.

Commodities transported include grains, oilseeds, fertilizer, minerals, coal, steel and steel related products, aggregates, forest products, cement and liquids as oilseed oils, liquid fertilizer and others.  

In addition to our barging operations, our group of companies also operates terminals for loading and unloading barges at several locations along the Danube. These terminals handle a broad range of dry cargo commodities and provide an intermodal link between barges, trucks, and rail. At some of those locations we can offer packing and sacking operations, such as bulk to big packs or bulk to small bags below 50 kg's.

We also operate two shipyards to repair, maintain and new-build pushers, cargo-vessel and barges for our own needs but also for many other ship-owners.

 

TTS (Transport Trade Services) GmbH. in Austria is a subsidiary of TTS S.A., Bucharest, Romania.

We were set up in August 2014, established to link the Rhine with the Danube and are taking care of the nautically very difficult areas on the Upper Danube (Wildungsmauer, Kienstock and Pfelling).

We are promoting all services, offered by the network, to our customers on the Upper Danube section and act as an independent freight forwarder.
