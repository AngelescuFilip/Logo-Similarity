    Online shop

    The Würth Online Shop is an electronic catalog and ordering system for our customers that is accessible via the Internet. The online shop gives you access to more than 20,000 products, product details, approvals and data sheets. In addition, this platform offers the possibility of efficient ordering through the use of scanners.

    Other advantages are:
    • Simple article search, also via combinations of search criteria
    • Quick search and display of catalogue pages, product information, safety data sheets and your current, individual prices
    • Simple ordering by adding the desired items to the shopping cart and tracking
    • Installation and management of persons with rights allocation
    • Order service around the clock, every day of the week
    • Orders are immediately forwarded to our logistics department for delivery
    • Use and management of desired cost centers
    Logistics/Delivery

    Logistics and delivery as you need it! When ordering, you can decide whether you want the goods conveniently shipped to the company or to the construction site directly, or if you want to pick up the goods immediately via Click & Collect at the nearest branch. Subscription orders is the simple and process cost effective alternative. Simply subscribe to Würth products, receive regular deliveries, and reduce warehousing costs.

    Overview of our delivery options:
    • Standard delivery.
    • Click & Collect – order online, pick up at the branch office.
    Customer account customization

    With the function "My account" you can create individual user accounts with different rights, Budgets and Cost Centers. If several users are using the online shop, individual functionalities are useful and also allow the administrator to keep track of orders and expenses.

    • User administration with rights and role concept
    • Invoice and delivery note reprint
    • Cost center management and order value limits
    • Budgeting and approval workflow
    Professional features

    Would you like our shop for professional functions such as order templates, individual customer material numbers or for inventory? No problem! Because our online shop can do more! We are not only distinguished as the best B2B online shop, but also keep our promise and deliver free additional services.

    • Order templates at the customer or user level
    • Deposition of customer material numbers
    • Inventory function
    • Offer management
    Process optimization

    With electronic procurement you can optimize and profit from your procurement processes and thus from the more efficient and faster procurement of your products. Whether small parts or cost-intensive A-items, the internal time and cost expenditure is always the same. Especially with C-parts, the internal process costs exceed the actual value of the goods many times over. From the order confirmation to the acceptance of the goods and inspection of the delivery up to the comparison of the invoice, everything must be recorded and processed in the accounting system. In a manual process, this means it takes a lot of time - and time is money!

    Our solutions for your process optimization:
    • Order files in various formats such as UGL, UGS, Datanorm or Opentrans
    • Electronic invoice data formats
    • E-Procurement functions per punchout with e.g. OCI, IDS, Ariba, cXML or ComNorm
