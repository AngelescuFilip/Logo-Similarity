Helping the world communicate for over 120 years..

Berlitz Dublin has been providing quality Language education for over 120 years . Always at the forefront of innovation, We are committed to delivering professional, fun programmes which are designed to help you achieve your language learning goals as quickly and effectively as possible.

Within a warm and friendly Berlitz learning-environment. We understand that everyone is different and we look to help your personalise your Berlitz learning experience to achieve your learning goals. Whether you wish to study English to achieve business and career goals, or are looking to progress to a university of your choice.. or simply studying for the enjoyment. We have study- solutions for you! Whether you are looking for General English, IELTS preparation, Academic English or Professional English, make Berlitz Manchester your first choice!
