Helping the world communicate for over 120 years..

Berlitz Manchester has been providing quality Language education for over 120 years. Always at the forefront of innovation, we are committed to delivering professional, fun programmes which are designed to help you achieve your language learning goals as quickly and effectively as possible.

Within a warm and friendly Berlitz learning-environment, we understand that everyone is different and we look to help your personalise your learning experience to achieve your learning goals. Whether you wish to study English to achieve business and career goals, or are looking to progress to a university of your choice, we have study solutions for you!

If you are looking for General English, IELTS Preparation, Academic English or Professional English courses,

make Berlitz Manchester your first choice!
