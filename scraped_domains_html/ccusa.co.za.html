NEW FRIENDS. BEAUTIFUL LOCATIONS. INCREDIBLE EXPERIENCES.

For almost 40 years, CCUSA has sent hundreds of thousands of young people to camp, work, intern and volunteer experiences all around the world. We are an internationally recognized cultural exchange organization that offers several programs worldwide to participants from over 60 different countries. The time of your life is waiting for you . . .and CCUSA SOUTH AFRICA is here to make that happen.

Volunteer Programs

Volunteer from one week to several months in one of many countries around the world. There are multiple projects you can choose from, including environmental, children, health and human rights. If you want to make a difference then this program is for you.

Teacher Programs

Teacher USA allows qualified international teachers from around the world to teach full-time in accredited public and private primary and secondary schools (K – 12) in the United States.

Below is a small selection of the many programs available. 

Work Experience USA

Work Experience USA has earned a reputation as the premier J-1 international work program. With Work Experience USA, you can work during your summer vacation break from university (for up to 3 months). Participating on CCUSA’s Work Experience program this summer will allow you to work, live and sample the real “everyday” USA!
