Online shop

The Würth Online Shop is an electronic catalog and ordering system for our customers that is accessible via the Internet. The online shop gives you access to more than 28,000 products, product details, approvals and data sheets. In addition, this platform offers the possibility of efficient ordering through the use of scanners. Archives and shopping baskets can also be generated, budgets set up, cost centres managed and approval processes carried out. can be defined.

Other advantages are:
  • Simple article search, also via combinations of search criteria
  • Quick search and display of catalogue pages, product information, safety data sheets and your current, individual prices
  • Simple ordering by adding the desired items to the shopping cart and tracking
  • Installation and management of persons with rights allocation
  • Order service around the clock, every day of the week
  • Orders are immediately forwarded to our logistics department for delivery
  • Use and management of desired cost centers
  • Purchase on account, direct debit, Paypal, credit card
  • Services such as ISI!!, MSDS and more!
Logistics/Delivery

Logistics and delivery as you need it! When ordering, you can decide whether you want the goods conveniently shipped home, to the company or to the construction site directly, or if you want to pick up the goods immediately via Click and Collect at the nearest branch. All our deliveries are shipped via 24 hour service. This means, if you place your order by 16:00 the previous day, you can expect your goods on the next working day. We offer delivery tracking online by clicking on "My Wurth" and then clicking "Orders". We also offer subscription orders, to ensure a simple and cost effective alternative. Simply subscribe to Würth products, receive regular deliveries and reduce warehousing costs.

Overview of our delivery options:
  • Standard delivery with shipment tracking
  • Click & Collect – order online, pick up at the branch office
  • Regularly supplied with subscription orders

Process optimization

With electronic procurement you can optimize and profit from your procurement processes. and thus from the more efficient and faster procurement of your products. Whether small parts or cost-intensive A-items, the internal time and cost expenditure is always the same. Especially with C-parts, the internal process costs exceed the actual value of the goods many times over. From the order confirmation to the acceptance of the goods and inspection of the delivery up to the comparison of the invoice, everything must be recorded and processed in the accounting system. In a manual process, this means this takes a lot of time - and time is money!

Our solutions for your process optimization:
    • Order files in various formats such as UGL, UGS, Datanorm or Opentrans
    • Electronic invoice data formats
    • E-Procurement functions per punchout with e.g. OCI, IDS, Ariba, cXML or ComNorm
    • We also can be found on E-Commerce Market Places such as SAP Ariba, Meplato, Mercateo and more!

Get in touch to see if we can provide an E-Commerce solution for you via customer.services@wurth.co.uk
