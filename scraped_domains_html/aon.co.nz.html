At Aon, we exist to shape decisions for the better — to protect and enrich the lives of people around the world. In Aotearoa, New Zealand, this means supporting and protecting communities across the country.

United as one firm, we help our clients shape business decisions for the better. We believe that businesses thrive when the communities they serve and the people they employ also flourish; and we are committed to helping the communities that we operate in too. We provide our clients with advice and solutions that give them clarity and confidence to make better decisions – from business and personal insurance through to complex industry and risk consulting. Aon is in the business of Better Decisions.

Contact Us

Aon is proud of our national sponsorship partners: Surf Life Saving New Zealand, Spirit of Adventure Trust, and Sustainable Coastlines.

 

Across summer, surf lifeguards are not only keeping beaches safe but also showcasing their skills in a packed schedule of competitive sport and this year, we were proud to fly our flag with Surf Life Saving New Zealand at the 2025 Aon New Zealand Surf Lifesaving Championships in Tairāwhiti Gisborne. 

Simultaneously, our scholarship trainees are building valuable life skills on their voyages aboard Spirit of New Zealand, and our teams are actively participating in beach clean-ups with Sustainable Coastlines to ensure our shores are pristine for everyone to enjoy.
 



At Aon in Aotearoa, New Zealand, we believe that collectively, we can help build and protect resilient communities, preserve and nourish our precious coastlines, and support our future generations across the motu. We can’t wait to have you join us on the journey.
