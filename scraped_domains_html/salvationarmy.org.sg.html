ABOUT THE SALVATION ARMY

The Salvation Army, an international movement, is an evangelical part of the universal Christian Church. Its message is based on the Bible. Its ministry is motivated by the love of God. Its mission is to preach the gospel of Jesus Christ and to meet human needs in his name without discrimination.

The Salvation Army was established under the Statutes of Singapore, Ordinance 23 of 1939 as The Salvation Army Ordinance (Chapter 377). Our Unique Entity Number (UEN) is T07CC3012G. We have also been conferred the Institution of a Public Character (IPC) status and our IPC registration number is 000549.
