At Weidner Apartment Homes, we appreciate and care about our residents. Our focus on teamwork, communication, and excellence in service has created a distinct and exceptional culture in each of our communities in Canada including Alberta, British Columbia, Manitoba and Saskatchewan. Youll enjoy living in your thoughtfully designed apartment home with great amenities in a desirable location that provides easy access to all the modern shopping, dining and entertainment options the Great White North has to offer. Our culture

Since our founding in 1977, Weidner's driving motivator has been to improve residents' lives with professionally managed, fun, and comfortable apartment homes that allow them to thrive. We have a proven history of rapid, sustainable growth that provides Our People with stable careers. We don't just manage properties; we build communities! At Weidner, you're valued and are part of an exciting mission to provide quality living for all.
