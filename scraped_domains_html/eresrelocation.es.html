Solutions for Corporates

No relocation is like another and often the logistics are complex.

In eres we aim at bringing our client’s peace of mind, relieving your HR colleagues of the responsibilities involved with taking care of relocating employees, so they can focus on your core business activities.

 

Smooth and well-managed relocations contribute to higher employee satisfaction and retention rates. We make sure your employees feel supported. The depth and breadth of our experience allows us to serve all types of clients whether they are a small business or a large international company.

View Solution

Solutions for RMC / IMC

Thanks to our physical presence in 6 European countries, we are able to serve your clients and their transferees in a consistent way.

Reduces the time needed for due diligence and supply chain management on your end. We help you to manage potential risks by advising on local regulations, safety considerations, and potential challenges, allowing you to provide accurate information to your clients.

Eres Relocation Sevices is a Premium member of EuRA (European Relocation Association) and is bound by their ethics and Rules of Conduct. The stated aim of EuRA is to spread knowledge and understanding of the issues surrounding employee mobility. Further, EuRA enhances industry performance standards by communication and education.

Eres Relocation Services obtained the EuRA Global Quality Seal Plus on April 8th, 2013.
Based on an ISO 9001 process management model, the EuRA QCS+ specifies the processes and KPIs, which reflect the very highest standards in relocation services. As a Premium member awarded with the EuRA Global Quality Seal Plus, Eres Relocation Services demonstrates a total commitment to quality.
