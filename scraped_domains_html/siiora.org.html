Orthopedic Implants

Orthopedic Implants are used for the fixation of different types of fractures and the treatment of other bone-related conditions surgically. They are fabricated using ISO standard titanium and stainless steel for being biocompatible and structurally strong. SIORA SURGICALS is a reliable manufacturer of international standard quality orthopedic implants that are trusted by some of the top players in the orthopedic industry across the globe. Trauma implants for orthopedic surgeries manufactured by SIORA are checked on stringent quality parameters before being approved safe for use.

Orthopedic Implants Manufacturers in India

Trusted by hundreds of global clients and backed by a team of hardworking & diligent professionals, SIORA SURGICALS is proud to be crowned as one of the best companies in India that manufacture high-quality orthopedic implants. The company is operating for more than three decades and this experience has helped SIORA establish a reputable position in the global market. The FDA India-approved manufacturing unit of SIORA is located in Sonipat, Haryana, while its corporate office is based in Delhi. All the implants used for trauma surgery manufactured by SIORA conforms to WHO-GMP, ISO 9001:2015, and ISO 13485:2016 standards.

Above all, the manufactured orthopedic implants and instruments are packed in an ISO class 7 (10,000) cleanroom environment to avoid any microbial contamination.

Orthopedic Implants Company in India

It might not be easy for someone to find a reliable company in India that manufactures international standard quality orthopedic implants, but with SIORA SURGICALS, you need not search any other company. Having hundreds of trusted clients globally, SIORA has always maintained healthy and fruitful relationships with all its distributors and suppliers based in different countries. Not only as a reliable manufacturer of trauma implants, but the company has also established its reputable position as a trustworthy orthopedic implants supplier to different corners of the world. With all this, SIORA SURGICALS is also available for providing OEM/contract manufacturing services for orthopedic implants and instruments.
