            Shop our affordable New and Pre-Owned models at Nissan of Lebanon

            At Nissan of Lebanon, we work hard to provide an excellent variety of new and used Nissan models to meet whatever transportation needs you have. We've been serving families here in New Hampshire for many years and we can provide you with whatever Nissan model and body style you're looking for.

            If you're not quite ready for a brand-new Nissan, we offer a variety of pre-owned vehicles from Nissan and other trusted brands. These pre-owned vehicles are popular and sell quickly but you can look to us for a value-priced version of the best-selling Nissan Rogue or other Nissan models. We also offer quality used models from other trusted brands like Toyota, Honda, Volvo, Jeep, Subaru and Kia.

            Save on a new Nissan Sentra sedan

            The Nissan Sentra gives you enduring sedan styling in a compact car along with a spacious, well-built interior. The Sentra also provides a long list of standard features and has enough seats in the cabin for up to five people. The new Nissan Sentra comes with standard front-wheel drive and delivers amazing fuel efficiency, both in town and on the highway. You'll find this is another new Nissan that will fit comfortably into your budget.

            Get a great price on a new Nissan Altima sedan

            If you're looking for a spacious mid-size sedan, look no further than the new Nissan Altima. It comes with an infotainment system that's simple to use and it also has a reassuring list of standard safety features. The new Altima offers an available 12.3-inch touch screen and the outside has an attractive new design. The new Nissan Altima is also attractive in terms of pricing and economical when it comes to fuel efficiency.

            Drive home in an affordable new Nissan Kicks SUV

            For those who want a value-priced subcompact SUV that can help you navigate city life, the new Nissan Kicks might be just what you're looking for. It has a surprising amount of passenger and cargo space and it's equipped with an abundance of standard features. The new Nissan Kicks is the smallest and most affordable SUV option that Nissan makes and it keeps saving you money with excellent fuel efficiency.

            Trim your costs with a new Nissan Versa sedan

            The new Nissan Versa has a well-built cabin and the kind of dynamic handling that makes driving enjoyable. This subcompact sedan provides an abundance of advanced technology features and the trunk is larger than you might expect. What's more, the Nissan Versa will give you outstanding fuel efficiency. Best of all, the new Nissan Versa is the most inexpensive new car you can buy in America.

            Stay in your comfort zone with a new Nissan Murano

            The new Nissan Murano is a mid-size SUV that gives your family a comfortable interior, a powerful V6 engine and a smooth ride. Seats provide plenty of comfort and the interior is very attractive. In addition, the entertainment system is simple to use and the suspension system provides an enjoyable ride. The new Murano manages to give you enough power on the highway and still provide solid fuel efficiency.

            The new Nissan Frontier pickup

            Are you looking for a mid-size pickup that can help you get a lot done on the job or around the house? The new Nissan Frontier provides a powerful V6 engine with 310 horsepower and it's available with either rear-wheel drive or four-wheel drive. Ask us about getting the rugged Nissan Frontier Pro-4X off-road version with Bilstein dampers and skid plates.

            We'll Help You get the Right Financing

            Our financing team is knowledgeable about all your financing choices and we can explain them in a simple, straightforward manner. Visit our finance center or contact us so we can help you decide whether to get a loan or a lease and then help you drive home with the best available rate. You can get approved in advance by using our secure online financing application.

            Service After the Sale

            We have Nissan factory-trained service technicians on staff who can give your new or used Nissan the regular maintenance it needs for a long and healthy life. We service all makes and models and we use advanced diagnostic equipment to make sure the job is done right. We'll also make sure you get genuine Nissan parts for any Nissan repair we do for you. Shop our new or used inventory and schedule a test drive soon at Nissan of Lebanon.
