The distinctive profile of your building

Achieve individuality and uniqueness by creatively using our StoSignature system: this is how the facade of a day-care centre in Frankfurt am Main got its distinctive appearance. With the aid of simple templates, a second, rough layer was precisely applied to parts of the fine render layer. The verdict of the architects dirschl.federle_architekten: “We should do this more often!” We think so too: familiarise yourself with the sophisticated StoSignature system including 5 texture families and additional effect materials such as metallic coatings, granulates, and lasures.

The StoSignature system

Discover numerous, clearly structured combination possibilities which you can use to creatively put together your facade project, any way you like in line with our formula:

Textures + effects = endless possibilities

Textures: The Textures category includes rendered surfaces in the Fine, Rough, Linear, and Graphic groups.

Effects: In the Effects category, these textures can be customised to an even greater extent with render (2.Textures), colour coatings (Coatings), or Granulates applied to the full surface (Total), partially (Partial), or using a stencil (Defined).

Endless possibilities: No matter what kind of design you have in mind, the StoSignature modular system offers a multitude of combination options which will help bring your ideas to life.

Impressions: The Impressions category includes finished texture and effect combinations in metal, timber, and concrete effects.
