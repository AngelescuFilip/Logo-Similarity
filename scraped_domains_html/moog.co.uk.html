About Moog in the United Kingdom

 

For over 50 years, Moogs UK facilities have been instrumental in developing cutting-edge motion control technology. From becoming the leading global supplier of servo valves to designing unique aerospace components, test systems, and miniature actuation systems for Formula 1, our people make the impossible possible.

Across the UK, we have recently invested in brand new production facilities in Tewkesbury, Wolverhampton, and Reading where we manufacture advanced systems for complex aerospace, defence, space, and industrial applications, where precise control of velocity, force, acceleration, and fluid flow are critical. We believe in the people who work for us, and offer apprenticeships and internships, encouraging young people to enter the world of engineering.

And through our culture, create an atmosphere of trust and confidence that inspires the development of incredible solutions to extraordinary challenges.

Our Purpose

As global leaders in motion control across land, sea, air, and space, we pioneer
advanced solutions, including both hardware and software, to drive innovation in customer
products. Our dedication and cutting-edge technology positively impact millions, playing a vital
role in creating a healthier, safer, and more sustainable world.

We take a collaborative approach to solving our customers most difficult motion control problems with electric, hydraulic and electro-hydrostatic solutions. We seek to develop long-term partnerships. We are a global organisation, yet we maintain a close customer-focused approach through local support.
