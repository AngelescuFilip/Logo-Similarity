What protection do you need for your business?

Many business owners have shared similar stories where their cash flow has been impacted. If scenarios such as these affect you, the good news is, we can help.

Debt Resolution

What changes are you seeing in your clients payment behaviour? Have you been spending too much time chasing slow payers? How would you like that time back to spend on whats important? We can help make that happen. >>>

Terms & Conditions

When your customer defaults on payment, are you covered? Do you have Terms that stipulate how and when you need to be paid and any variations that may occur? We can implement/ update your Terms to help protect you from loss of profit. >>>

Securing your assets

Do you hire out or have your unsecured equipment on your clients site? How would you be impacted if a client went into liquidation? We can set you up as a secured creditor and update your Terms to safeguard your goods and assets. >>>

Privacy solutions

 If a privacy breach were to happen, do you know what you need to do? If you collect customers personal information or have a website that does, we can put systems in place to make you compliant with legislation. >>>

Credit Reporting

Are you supplying goods and services or loan facilities to other businesses/people? Do you know their risk profile or if they have the ability to pay you? We can give you access to consumer and business credit reports so you are better informed. >>>
