VKF Renzel
With more than 25,000 sales promotion products

With more than 25,000 products, our online webshop offers solutions in all areas of sales promotion. The range includes classic promotional items such as brochure holders, poster stands, sidewalk signs, click frames and article carriers. We are also your specialist in the field of advertising items, digital signage, exhibition supplies (including information desks and barriers), presentation displays and various products for shop design, shelf presentations and price labeling. Benefit from our high availability and customized packaging units. Even for small quantities, we provide standard products with an advertising message or your logo.

In addition to plastic, we also process wood, acrylic glass, aluminum and metal in our products.
In short: your presentation is our specialization!
