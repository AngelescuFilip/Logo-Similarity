Justice. Compassion. Love.

In more than 100 countries we provide water, sanitation, health, food, education, and income opportunities so that all may live as God intended. We are the official humanitarian agency of the Seventh-day Adventist Church. Since 1985, we’ve strived to serve humanity, end extreme poverty, and deliver emergency relief.
