Everything in Bathrooms since 1917

With over 100 years of experience Roca has become an international leader in design and production of bathroom products. Quality, innovation, design and sustainability are at the heart of all Roca products.

Toilets

Roca toilets are synonymous with guarantee and quality thanks to our century-old history linked to handcrafted excellence in the production of vitreous china. Choose from a wide array of designs: two-piece toilets, wall-hung toilets, single floorstanding toilets and In-Tank toilets.

Furniture

Roca bathroom furniture, manufactured with the most resistant materials and complying with the most demanding quality standards, offers design, functionality and comfort in a large variety of proposals for all styles and storage needs. Discover our range of base units, vanity units, auxiliary units and complements.

Faucets

Roca faucets are examples of high quality and durability thanks to the use of the best raw materials and Roca's exclusive alloys and formulations. Choose from a wide range of designs and highly aesthetic and functional solutions for your bathroom or kitchen.

Shower trays

Roca shower trays, manufactured with top-quality materials, combine aesthetic and safety in a wide range of shapes, materials, styles and finishes for your bathroom.

Baths

Roca baths have been designed in compliance with the most demanding quality and durability standards in a wide selection of styles, materials and shapes. Find out the Roca bath that best adapts to your style, from classic to contemporary models and with different hydromassage options.

Showers

Roca shower solutions provide a customized experience thanks to their combination of hand-showers, shower-heads, complements, jets, shower sets and kits.

Accessories

Roca accessories add personality to your bathroom with small details that make a difference. Choose from a great variety of accessories available for installation with adhesive or screws.
