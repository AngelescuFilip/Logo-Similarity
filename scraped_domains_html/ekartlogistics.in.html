    Logistics Solutions

    B2C EXPRESS

    Integrated solution with Surface and Air Express services to fulfil all e-commerce business needs. Innovative solutions around Same Day Delivery, Next Day Delivery and One Step Exchange to products with multiple payment options enhance the doorstep experience.

    WAREHOUSING

    Flexible, affordable and scalable warehousing and inventory management backed by our pan India presence of Grade A warehouses. With our extended warehouse solution we provide customers with services that enable them to further add value to the warehousing operations.
