Our Services

When the fundamentals are taken care of, you can focus on what matters: growing your business, not your to-do list.

  • Audit & Assurance

    Quality driven, high standard, constructive and intelligent. Our team offers Audit and Assurance Services in line with highest ethical and professional standards and professional behavior.

  • Accounting services

    Accounting and financial reporting services in accordance with Law of accounting and auditing in Bosnia and Herzegovina, including salary calculations, tax applications and financial reporting.

  • Tax services

    Our team offers general on demand consultations regarding VAT and DIRECT TAX including representation and consulting organisations with TAX inspections.

  • Business consultancy

    Financial consultancy services includes financial analysis, plans for establishment, reorganizations, liquidation process, guidance on application of legislative regulating business environment in Bosnia and Herzegovina.

  • VAT representation

    VAT Representation services for foreign entities performing business in Bosnia and Herzegovina in accordance with VAT Law of Bosnia and Herzegovina.

Industries

Our experienced team assists owners and executives across industries in enhancing their operations through a comprehensive range of services.

Construction

Our services include financial audits, VAT representation services, registration of business units, as well as accounting, administrative, and other related services. Our goal is to ensure smooth operations for our clients by providing support throughout all project phases.

Manufacturing

With years of experience in the manufacturing sector, we help companies optimize their business processes, manage finances, and comply with relevant legislation. Our team provides advisory services for more efficient planning and implementation of growth strategies.

Energy and Renewables

Baker Tilly has knowledge and experience in the energy sector, including renewable energy projects. We help clients with feasibility analyses, business audits, and accounting operations. We particularly emphasize our services in ESG standards compliance.
