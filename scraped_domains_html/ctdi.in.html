About CTDI

CTDI India is a fully owned subsidiary of Communications Test Design Inc. (CTDI), headquartered in West Chester, Pennsylvania, USA. CTDI is a global service provider specialized in Repair, engineering and logistics. CTDI was founded in the family garage of Parsons family in 1975 and continues to be run as a family business. The company has 100+ facilities worldwide spread across 23 countries and has over 20,000 employees.
