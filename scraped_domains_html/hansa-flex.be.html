Hose Line Management

Preventive maintenance of hydraulic systems and machines can be so simple - with the My.HANSA-FLEX customer portal. 
 
By marking them with our well-known X-CODE, you can view all hose assemblies online and maintain your data yourself. Define maintenance and inspection intervals, arrange service appointments and have visual inspections carried out meeting legal requirements.
 
We will be happy to advise you on the subject of preventive maintenance and hose management without obligation and show you our demo version.
