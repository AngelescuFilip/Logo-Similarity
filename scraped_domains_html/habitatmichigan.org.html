Helping Build & Restore  Michigan Communities

Welcome to Habitat for Humanity of Michigan, where we are dedicated to helping build and restore communities across the state. Habitat’s vision is a world where everyone has a decent place to live. To us, that starts in Michigan. Michigan has a robust network of 45 Habitat affiliates, serving almost every county in the state. Habitat for Humanity of Michigan is here to support the work your local Michigan affiliate is doing on the ground in your community.

What We Do

We represent 45 Habitat For Humanity affiliates across Michigan. Through leadership, advocacy, and education, Habitat for Humanity of Michigan has helped thousands of families improve their everyday lives.

Homebuyer Counseling & Financial Coaching

Habitat Michigan, a HUD-approved agency, employs certified counselors and coaches to assist Michigan residents in enhancing their financial and housing situations, guiding and supporting them in their journey toward homeownership while eliminating obstacles along the way.

Home Repair Programs

Habitat affiliates in Michigan receive funding through partnership grants to collaborate with families needing urgent and costly critical repairs in their homes, prioritizing safety, health, and security by assessing and ranking needs along a critical path, ensuring timely completion of essential steps to address safety and health issues affecting many Michigan families.

Veterans Housing Programs

Habitat for Humanity of Michigan offers housing services for veterans, providing resources for critical home repairs, home modifications for disabilities, and homeowner assistance programs.

Lending Programs

Habitat for Humanity of Michigan, through its subsidiary, Fair Lending Services, offers accessible small dollar loans as an alternative to “payday loans”. In addition, we facilitate connections to specific mortgage programs to empower individuals in securing affordable housing solutions.

Advocate

Through collaborative efforts with local communities, policymakers, and volunteers, Habitat for Humanity of Michigan actively works to address housing challenges, promote equitable solutions, and empower families with the stability and opportunity that a secure home provides.

Who We Are

Let’s build a world where everyone has a decent place to live. At Habitat Michigan, we think it’s possible, but we believe change needs to happen in our own neighborhood first. That’s why we provide programs, education, and resources to strengthen Michigan communities. We speak for 45 affiliates across the state that are bringing hope and shelter to Michigan families. HFHM cultivates an environment that reflects the values and needs of our community by seeking to serve, welcome and empower people of every race, ethnicity, nationality, economic status, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, size, and ability.
