Welcome to PKF Lesotho

 

PKF Lesotho (Chartered Accountants and Business Advisers) is a member firm of PKF International. With representation in 150 countries, consisting of over 200 members with more than 500 offices. PKF International provides a highly-ranked global network of local expertise in audit, accounting and business advisory.

We specialise in advising growing and entrepreneurial/owner-managed businesses, listed companies, public institutions, local authorities and not-for-profit organisations. We pride ourselves on offering world class knowledge combined with the resources and expertise of an international network.

Our aim is to work closely with our clients to understand their goals and so help to achieve them. PKF clients globally are drawn from every conceivable industry, and range from start-ups, family businesses, and small companies to medium companies.
