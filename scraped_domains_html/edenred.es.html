Nuestros compromisos sociales

Mejorar la calidad de vida

Buscamos ser un empleador de referencia en términos de desarrollo profesional, derechos humanos y diversidad, promoviendo el bienestar mediante una alimentación saludable y sostenible y contribuyendo al desarrollo local.

Conservar el medio ambiente

Trabajamos para reducir nuestra huella de carbono, consumo de recursos y residuos, diseñando servicios ecológicos para la movilidad y contra el desperdicio de alimentos y controlando el impacto de nuestras soluciones.

Crear valor de forma responsable

Nos comprometemos a desarrollar nuestras actividades y asociaciones de manera ética en toda su cadena de valor. Desde el grupo Edenred garantizamos la seguridad informática y la protección de datos, al tiempo que cumplimos con las expectativas de nuestros clientes y usuarios y los involucramos en ladigitalización.

Strictly Necessary Cookies

Always Active

Necessary cookies maintain the site and cannot be disabled. The cookies are used e.g. in functions related to service requests, data protection settings, logins, and filling in forms. You can block them in your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of the site. Necessary cookies do not store any personally identifiable information.

Performance Cookies

With the help of performance cookies, we count the number of visits to our site and track different traffic sources. This way we can measure and improve the site's performance and understand the usage of the pages. All information these cookies collect is aggregated and therefore anonymous. By rejecting these cookies, we cannot develop our site based on your activity.

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies offer a more individual user experience by using e.g. videos and real-time chat. These cookies are set by us or by our third-party service provider. By rejecting these cookies, some functions may not work properly.

Targeting Cookies

These cookies are set on our site by our advertising partners. They can use cookies to recognize the browser and the device you use, collect information about your interests, and show you targeted content. If you reject these cookies, we will not be able to target the most relevant ads to you.

Social Media Cookies

Our site has been set with social media cookies, which track your browser activity across different sites and collect information about your interests. This may affect the content and messages created for you. By rejecting these cookies, you may not be able to use all of our site’s functions.
