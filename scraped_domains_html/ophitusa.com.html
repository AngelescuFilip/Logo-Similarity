WHO ARE WE

Global Sales, Support and Service

Ophit’s global product line comprises optical extenders, multi-format video converters, splitters, switches, multi-scaler viewers, and matrix switchers. We’re committed to the principles of Total Quality Leadership to meet and exceed customers’ expectations. – ISO 9001 certified. 

As an industry pioneer for over two decades, we’ve established ourselves as a leading manufacturer of cutting-edge audio-video products. our broad-based connectivity solutions include Operating Room, Media & Entertainment, Manufacturing, Pro-AV, Corporate, CCTV Security Surveillance, Teleconferencing, Transportation, Government, Healthcare, and K-12 Education.
